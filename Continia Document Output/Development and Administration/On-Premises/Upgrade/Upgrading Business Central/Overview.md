# Upgrading NAV/Business Central with Continia Document Output Installed

If you use Document Output in an old version of Microsoft Dynamics Business Central and want to upgrade Business Central to a later version, you must also upgrade Document Output and/or carry out related tasks, depending on what version you use.

The articles below provide guidelines as to what exact steps you must take in such scenarios. To access the guidelines that are relevant to you, simply pick the version of Business Central that you want to upgrade to:

* [Business Central 2025 Release Wave 1 (v26)](@DO-220)
* [Business Central 2024 Release Wave 2 (v25)](@DO-198)
* [Business Central 2024 Release Wave 1 (v24)](@DO-197)
* [Business Central 2023 Release Wave 2 (v23)](@DO-196)
