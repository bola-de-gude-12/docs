# Enabling Digital Vouchers for Business Central v14-v22 in Document Output
To set up Document Output to automatically create digital vouchers for older versions of Business Central (BC v14 to BC v22), follow these steps:

1. Choose the {{search}} icon, enter **Document Output Setup**, and then choose the related link.
1. On the **Digital Vouchers** FastTab, enable the **Enable digital vouchers** toggle.

For all sales documents that are registered, Document Output will now create digital vouchers automatically according to your setup.
