# Support for Service Invoices and Service Credit Notes in XML

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Support for service invoices and service credit notes in XML | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2021  | - |

## Business value

Service invoices and service credit notes can now be generated as XML files.

## Feature details

The document types service invoice and service credit note can now be generated as PEPPOL BIS3/XRechnung invoices or credit notes. With this feature, it's now possible to send these documents through the Continia Delivery Network.
