# Proforma Invoices

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Proforma invoice templates | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2021 |  {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2021  | - |

## Business value
It's now possible to create proforma invoice templates.

## Feature details
Proforma invoices are now supported. This enhances the versatility of the supported invoice document types and gives you more invoicing flexibility because you can create draft invoices, invoices, and proforma invoices.
