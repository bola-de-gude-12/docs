# Greater Flexibility When Attaching Files

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Greater flexibility when attaching files | {{checkmark}} Apr 19, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Apr 19, 2021  | - |

##Feature details
Attaching files to emails will become more flexible, for example when there are line attachments in sales orders, purchase orders, quotes or similar. It will also be possible to attach external documents.
