# Different Background Images in PDF Files

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Different background images in PDF files | {{checkmark}} Mar 2023 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2023 |

## Business value
This feature expands on the [Setting a Background PDF](@DO-45) feature and gives you more flexibility when you want to add company information, such as logos or watermarks, to background PDF files.

## Feature details

With this feature, it's possible to have a unique first page and a unique last page in background PDF images consisting of multiple pages. Before, it was only possible to select one image as background per email template line.

You'll find more information in the full article [Setting a Background PDF ](@DO-45)
