# PEPPOL Bis3 AUNZ Variant

| Feature | Public preview | General availability on-premises | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| PEPPOL Bis3 AU/NZ variant | - | {{checkmark}} Jun 2022 | {{checkmark}} Jun 2022 |

## Business value
With this feature, The AU/NZ variant of PEPPOL Bis3 will meet the requirements of the PEPPOL authority in AU/NZ so you can continue to send the document types invoices and credit notes using Delivery Network to government customers in these countries after July 1, 2022, where the PEPPOL Bis3 AU/NZ variant becomes mandatory when sending invoices/credit notes to government customers.

## Feature details
The format will meet the mandatory requirements from the PEPPOL authorities in Australia and New Zealand.
