# Password Protect PDF

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Password protect PDF | - | {{checkmark}} Oct 2022 |

## Business value
Protection of the content of PDF files sent between you and your customers, heightening security and strengthening privacy in your business.

## Feature details
With this feature, it will be possible to lock PDF files with a password that can then be shared with the document receiver. This will only be applicable for templates when sending emails.
