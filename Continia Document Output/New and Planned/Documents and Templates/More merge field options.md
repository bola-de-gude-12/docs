# More Merge Field Options

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| More merge field options | {{checkmark}} Mar 2024 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2024 |

## Business value

This feature makes it easy to use merge fields in email templates, saving you time and effort. 

Merge field numbers can be difficult to remember, and hundreds might be available, so it can be a slow process to find the right one – and on top of that remember the ID. This feature dispenses with any difficulties of that kind.
