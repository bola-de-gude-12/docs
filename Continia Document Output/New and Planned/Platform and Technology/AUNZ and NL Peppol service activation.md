# AU/NZ and NL Peppol Service Activation

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| AU/NZ and NL Peppol service activation | {{checkmark}} Mar 2024 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2024 |

## Business value
This feature widens the application area of Document Output in The Netherlands, Australia and New Zealand for Business Central users because they will be able to use the Continia Delivery Network to send the document types invoices and credit notes.

## Feature details
The format will meet the mandatory requirements from the PEPPOL authorities in Australia and New Zealand, which take effect on July 1, 2022.

You can find further information in the following articles:
* [Onboarding to the Continia Delivery Network](@DO-94)
* [Electronic invoicing in New Zealand](@DC-201)
