# NemHandel Integration (DK)

| Feature | Public preview | General availability on-premises | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| NemHandel integration (DK) | - | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 |

## Business value
With this feature, documents in the OIOUBL format can be send directly to NemHandel operators in Denmark. This makes it possible to send documents to customers that aren't using the PEPPOL format yet.

## Feature details
OIOUBL is the legacy electronic invoice format in Denmark, and it's used by businesses that are not yet ready to use PEPPOL (meaning that they can’t receive PEPPOL Bis3 invoices or credit notes).
