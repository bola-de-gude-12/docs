# Enhanced In-App Notification Groups

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Enhanced In-App Notification Groups | - | Oct 2023 |

## Business value
With the introduction of Continia Notifications earlier in 2023, it is important to ensure that notifications regarding the usage of the Delivery Network are specifically targeted to customers based on their network usage (sending, receiving, or both). This update aims to improve the accuracy of notifications by targeting the right audience.

## Feature details
We are implementing additional parameters to enhance further the effectiveness of Continia Notifications related to the usage of the Delivery Network. These parameters will enable even more precise targeting of notifications, ensuring that customers receive the most relevant information based on their specific network usage requirements if necessary.
