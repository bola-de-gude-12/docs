# Automatic AL Extensions in Custom-Made Email Templates

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Automatic AL extensions for custom-made email templates | - | {{checkmark}} Oct 2023 |

## Business value

Document Output offers over 35 email templates that can be easily set up. This feature can be used in various areas of Business Central, not just limited to the 35 document types provided. It also allows for customization to support new document types that businesses may need. This feature is available for both on-premise and cloud solutions.

## Feature details

When you create your own custom templates, it's a difficult and complex process to manually create all the AL extensions needed to make the templates work the same way as the standard Document Output templates. This feature will make the process smoother and enable developers to easily create new templates and make them ready for use.
