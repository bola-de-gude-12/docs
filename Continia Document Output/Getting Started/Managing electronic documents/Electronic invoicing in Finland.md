# Managing Electronic Documents in Finland
As a customer in Finland, you can send electronic documents either by email (as attached files) or through the PEPPOL eDelivery Network. The Finvoice and TEAPPSXML formats have been widely adopted by both businesses and public authorities in Finland, but currently Document Output doesn't support any of these formats.
