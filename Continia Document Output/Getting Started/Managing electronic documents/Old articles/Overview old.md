# Sending Electronic Documents
Sending electronic documents directly from Continia Document Output is the easiest way to be compliant and to optimize your business. More and more public authorities all over the world are mandating electronic exchange of documents, and this is also becoming increasingly popular among business-to-business organizations, which is why it's an essential part of Document Output.

Your options for exporting and sending electronic documents with Document Output depend on what types of electronic documents and files you're sending. You can export and send files from Document Output either by email or by connecting directly to the Continia Delivery Network.

The Continia Delivery Network is a service that integrates with the PEPPOL eDelivery Network, thereby enabling you to send electronic sales invoices and credit memos directly from Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. Continia is a certified PEPPOL access point, which eliminates the need for any third-party vendors to integrate your company into the PEPPOL eDelivery Network. When you sign up to the Continia Delivery Network, you're automatically set up and enabled to send documents to all recipients in the global PEPPOL eDelivery Network.
