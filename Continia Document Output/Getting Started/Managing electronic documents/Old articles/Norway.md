# Sending Electronic Documents in Norway
As a customer in Norway, you can send electronic documents either by email (as attached files) or through the PEPPOL eDelivery Network. To promote the general adoption of PEPPOL, the Norwegian authorities mandate that all public authorities must be able to receive electronic documents through the PEPPOL eDelivery Network. Document Output can send to all public authorities in the PEPPOL BIS 3 format, which is the latest standard.

The EHF format has been widely adopted by both businesses and public authorities in Norway. The EHF 3 format is basically the same as the PEPPOL BIS 3 format, and Document Output supports this. Earlier versions of the EHF format are currently not supported.

Continia Document Output connects you directly to the PEPPOL eDelivery Network, thereby eliminating the need to sign up with any third parties for integration. Once connected, you can start sending electronic documents to your customers in Norway and other countries. To learn more about how to connect to the PEPPOL eDelivery Network, see [Setting up the Continia Delivery Network](@DO-12).

You can also export and send electronic documents to customers in other countries. Most widespread formats are supported, but not all. For a list of supported formats, see [Supported Formats](@DO-57).
