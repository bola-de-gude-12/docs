# Sending Electronic Documents in Sweden
As a customer in Sweden, you can send electronic documents either by email (as attached files) or through the PEPPOL eDelivery Network.

Continia Document Output connects you directly to the PEPPOL eDelivery Network, thereby eliminating the need to sign up with any third parties for integration. Once connected, you can start sending electronic documents to your customers in Sweden and other countries. To learn more about how to connect to the PEPPOL eDelivery Network, see [Setting up the Continia Delivery Network](@DO-12).

To promote the general adoption of PEPPOL, the Swedish authorities mandate that all public authorities must be able to receive electronic documents through the PEPPOL eDelivery Network. Document Output can send to all public authorities in the PEPPOL BIS 3 format, which is the latest standard.

The Svefaktura format has been widely adopted by both businesses and public authorities in Sweden, but this is expected to eventually be replaced by PEPPOL. Currently, Document Output doesn't support Svefaktura.

You can also export and send electronic documents to customers in other countries. Most widespread formats are supported, but not all. For a list of supported formats, see [Supported Formats](@DO-57).
