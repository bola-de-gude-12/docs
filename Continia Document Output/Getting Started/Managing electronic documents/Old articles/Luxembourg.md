# Sending Electronic Documents in Luxemburg
As a customer in Luxemburg, you can send electronic documents either by email (as attached files) or through the PEPPOL eDelivery Network. Peppol BIS3 serves as the official e-invoicing format for both B2G  (Business-to-Government) invoicing and B2B (Business-to-Business)  invoicing in Luxembourg.

Continia Document Output connects you directly to the PEPPOL eDelivery Network, eliminating the need to sign up with any third parties for integration. Once connected, you can send electronic documents to your customers in Luxemburg and other countries. To learn more about how to connect to the PEPPOL eDelivery Network, see [Setting up the Continia Delivery Network](@DO-12).

You can also export and send electronic documents to customers in other countries. Most widespread formats are supported, but not all. For a list of supported formats, see [Supported Formats](@DO-57).
