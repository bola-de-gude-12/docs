# Sending Electronic Documents in Finland
As a customer in Finland, you can send electronic documents either by email (as attached files) or through the PEPPOL eDelivery Network. The Finvoice and TEAPPSXML formats have been widely adopted by both businesses and public authorities in Finland. Currently, Document Output doesn't support any of these formats.

Continia Document Output connects you directly to the PEPPOL eDelivery Network, thereby eliminating the need to sign up with any third parties for integration. Once connected, you can start sending electronic documents to your customers in Finland and other countries. To learn more about how to connect to the PEPPOL eDelivery Network, see [Setting up the Continia Delivery Network](@DO-12).



You can also export and send electronic documents to customers in other countries. Most widespread formats are supported, but not all. For a list of supported formats, see [Supported Formats](@DO-57).
