# Managing Electronic Documents
Sending electronic documents with Continia Document Output is the straightforward way to optimize your business. More and more public authorities all over the world are mandating electronic exchange of documents, and this is also becoming increasingly popular among business-to-business organizations, which is why it's the core of Document Output.

Your options for sending electronic documents with Document Output depend on which types of electronic documents and files you're sending. You can easily send files by connecting to the Continia Delivery Network.

The Continia Delivery Network is a service that integrates with the Peppol eDelivery Network, thereby enabling you to send and receive electronic documents directly in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. Continia is a certified Peppol access point, which eliminates the need for any third-party vendors to integrate your company into the Peppol eDelivery Network. When you sign up to the Continia Delivery Network, you're automatically set up as a recipient in the global Peppol eDelivery Network. To learn more about how to connect to the Peppol eDelivery Network or the NemHandel network, see [Setting up the Continia Delivery Network](@DO-12).  

You can send documents in a number of formats when using Document Output. Most widespread formats are supported, but not all. For a list of supported formats, see [Supported Formats](Supported Formats.md).
