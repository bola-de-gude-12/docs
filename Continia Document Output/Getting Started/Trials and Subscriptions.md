# Continia Document Output Trials and Subscriptions
You can use Document Output in several different ways, and some of these can even be combined. Each of the various methods is described in more detail below.

## Sign up for a trial

If you’d like to try out Document Output before subscribing, you can sign up for a free trial [here](https://appsource.microsoft.com/en-us/product/DynamicsBC/PUBID.continia365%7CAID.continia-document-output%7CPAPPID.f4b69b55-c90d-4937-8f53-2742898fa948) – simply select **Free trial** in the upper-left corner. This free trial gives you access to all Document Output functionality for up to 30 days or until the maximum number of free-trial documents have been processed. Following the trial period, or at any point during it, you can easily subscribe to Document Output if you want. You’ll already be up and running when you subscribe, and you can then continue working with the same data as in the trial period.

To get familiar with Document Output, see [Getting started with Document Output](Overview.md).

For any questions you might have, please reach out to your certified Continia partner. You can find more information [here](Resellers and Partners/Find a Reseller.md).

## Start a subscription

To get the full version of Document Output on a more permanent basis and with absolutely no limitations or strings attached, you can always subscribe and get started right away. In this way you can start benefiting from the app and using it for real sooner than with a trial, and the user-friendly interface makes it easy to get started.

To subscribe to Document Output, simply reach out to your existing Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central partner. Your partner will help you with all subscription-related matters and get you up and running fast. In fact, it’s important that you’re assisted by your partner when you activate any version of Document Output, as this ensures that you get off to a good start and that everything is done properly. For more information, see [Finding a Reseller of Document Output](Resellers and Partners/Find a Reseller.md).

You can ask your partner any questions you might have. If you’re looking for further inspiration or would like to familiarize yourself with Document Output, there’s plenty of useful information under [Getting started with Document Output](Overview.md).

## Set up a sandbox

Looking for a safe way to test and try out things in Document Output? Then a sandbox may be the way to go for you. Sandboxes let you practice and experiment without the risk of losing any valuable data or breaking anything in production, and you can process documents in them completely free of charge. 

Your administrator can set up one or more sandbox accounts alongside your production environment, even if you’ve signed up for a trial. No data is transferred from any of the sandbox accounts to your production environment, so playing around with sandbox data is completely risk-free.

To get started with sandboxes, please contact your existing Business Central partner. For more information, see [Finding a Reseller of Document Output](Resellers and Partners/Find a Reseller.md).
