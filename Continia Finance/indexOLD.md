# Welcome to Continia Finance

Continia Finance offers a range of modules that aim to simplify and enhance your financial tasks within Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. Our objective is to empower you by providing separate, focused modules that address specific finance-related needs, while seamlessly integrating into your existing workflow.

With Continia Finance, you can select individual modules that assist directly with various tasks, such as tracking associations, creating new assets, and generating open entries for General Ledger accounts. These separate add-ons help you tackle specific challenges, making your life easier.

Continia Finance provides you with the freedom to choose from our range of distinct modules, each serving a unique purpose.

* **Essential** - designed to simplify important actions like using fast posting codes, assigning VAT keys, and managing balance confirmation. This module is available to you for free.
* **Associations** - tracking associations and memberships becomes effortless with this module. Maintain clear records of member dues, contributions, and related financial transactions without missing a beat.
* **G/L Open Entries** - this module lets you generate open entries for General Ledger (G/L) accounts, similar to the process used for customers and vendors.
* **Extended Financial Reports** -  provides additional functionalities that extend customer and vendor entries with pages and special lists, allowing for easier year-end financial reporting.
* **Extended Fixed Assets** - helps you to define Fixed Assets templates, which allow you to speed up the process of creating new assets.
* **Installment Payments** - allows you to set up installment payment templates for different installment models so that you don't have to redefine how to split the payment over and over again. 
* **Multi-Level Payment Discounts** - helps you to apply additional discount tiers, each with its own discount percentage.
* **Treasury** - lets you view all payment-related data, including bank balances, open entries, payment import journals, payment proposals, and G/L entries across multiple companies.
