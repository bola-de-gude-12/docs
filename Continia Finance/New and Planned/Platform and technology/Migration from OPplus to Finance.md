# Migration from OPplus to Continia Finance

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Migration from OPplus to Finance | - | August 2025|

## Business value

This feature facilitates migration from OPplus to Continia Finance. This allows customers to seamlessly transition to Continia Finance to gain enhanced functionality and benefit from ongoing support, service packs, and hot fixes. This feature ensures a smooth migration process, enabling users to take advantage of the advanced capabilities and continuous updates provided by Continia Finance.

## Feature details

A new app will facilitate the migration from OPplus to Continia Finance. It will be available on demand, and when installed, it assists customers in transferring their data from OPplus to Finance, ensuring that all data previously available in OPplus is now accessible in Finance. The app handles the mapping and migration of OPplus tables to the corresponding Finance tables.
