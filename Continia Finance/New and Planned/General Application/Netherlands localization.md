# Support for Dutch Localization

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Support for Dutch localization | {{checkmark}} Mar 2025 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2025 |



## Business value

Having Continia Finance localized into Dutch makes it accessible to Dutch-speaking users.

## Feature details

With this change, Continia Finance will be available in the Netherlands, in the Dutch language, and with local Dutch Microsoft changes.
