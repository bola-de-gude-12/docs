# Continia Finance available in English in Italy and parts of Eastern Europe

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Continia Finance available in English in Italy and parts of Eastern Europe | - | October 2025 |



## Business value

Having Continia Finance available in English in Italy and multiple Eastern European countries makes it accessible to English-speaking users in these countries.

## Feature details

With this change, Continia Finance will be available in English in Italy, Czechia, Slovakia, Hungary, Poland, Serbia, and Bulgaria.
