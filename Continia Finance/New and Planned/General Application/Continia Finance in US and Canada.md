# Continia Finance in the USA and Canada

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |

## Business value

Customers in the USA and Canada will be able to make use of the features in Continia Finance to optimize workflows and reduce time spent on business administration.

## Feature details

Continia will open for access to Continia Finance to customers in the USA and Canada.
