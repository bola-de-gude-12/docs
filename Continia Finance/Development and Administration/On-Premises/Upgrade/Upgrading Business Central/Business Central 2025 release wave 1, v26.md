# Upgrading to Business Central 2025 Release Wave 1 (v26)

The following table provides an overview of what you have to do if you have Continia Finance (CF) installed and want to update your older version of Microsoft Dynamics Business Central (BC) to Business Central 2025 release wave 1 (v26). When you follow the guidelines given under **Tasks** below, Continia Finance remains fully functional and compatible with your updated version of Business Central.

<br>

| Scenario | Tasks |
| --- | --- |
| Full upgrade from the following versions: <ul><li>Business Central 2023 wave 1 (v22)</li><li>Business Central 2023 wave 2 (v23)</li><li>Business Central 2024 wave 1 (v24)</li><li>Business Central 2024 wave 2 (v25)</li></ul> | From these versions, you must do as follows to upgrade: <ol><li>Uninstall and unpublish all Continia apps.</li><li>Upgrade to BC v26 using [this Microsoft guide - link to be added later]. </li><li>Publish and install the latest versions of all Continia apps, using [this guide](@CF-22).</li></ol> |
