# Continia Finance Trials and Subscriptions

Try Continia Finance for free before subscribing - select "Free trial" in the upper-left corner. You'll have 30 days of access to all features. You can easily subscribe at any time. When you do, you'll have access to all features and can continue working with your trial data.

To get familiar with Continia Finance, see [Getting started with Continia Finance](Overview.md).

For any questions you might have, please reach out to your certified Continia partner. You can find more information [here](Resellers and Partners/Find a Reseller.md).

## Start a subscription

Subscribe for the full version of Continia Finance with no limitations or strings attached. It has a user-friendly interface, which makes it easy to get started and benefit from the app right away.

Reach out to your Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central partner to subscribe to Continia Finance. They'll help you with all subscription-related matters and ensure a proper start. For more information, see [Finding a Reseller of Continia Finance](Resellers and Partners/Find a Reseller.md).

You can ask your partner any questions you might have. If you’re looking for further inspiration or would like to familiarize yourself with Continia Finance, there’s plenty of useful information under [Getting started with Continia Finance](Overview.md).
