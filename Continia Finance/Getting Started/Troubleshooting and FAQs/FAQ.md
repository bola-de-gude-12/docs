# Frequently Asked Questions

In this section, you can find answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about Continia Finance. The answers provide information on anything from availability to zero-cost trials and also include multiple links to other sites with more information.

## Is Continia Finance available in my country?

Continia Finance is available in a range of countries and languages. You can find the full lists [here](@CF-128).

## How do I get Continia Finance?

To ensure that you get the best possible service, Continia operates through a network of highly specialized Continia partners. These partners, who are all official Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central partners, know everything there is to know about Continia Finance and Business Central in general and will be able to get you up and running in no time. Simply reach out to your dedicated Continia partner to get started right away!

See also: [Finding a reseller of Continia Finance](/Continia Finance/Getting Started/Resellers and Partners/Find a Reseller.md).

You can also start with a free 30-day trial or even set up a sandbox environment. For more information, see [Continia Finance Trials and Subscriptions](/Continia Finance/Getting Started/Trials and Subscriptions.md).

## Where do I go if I have questions?

If you have any questions about Continia Finance that you can't find answers to in this documentation, you’re more than welcome to ask your Continia partner.

For more information, see [Resources for Help and Support](/Continia Finance/Getting Started/Help and Support.md) and [Resellers and Partners](/Continia Finance/Getting Started/Resellers and Partners/Overview.md).

## What does it cost to use Continia Finance?

You can find detailed information on pricing for both online and on-premises usage [here](https://www.continia.com/pricing/).
