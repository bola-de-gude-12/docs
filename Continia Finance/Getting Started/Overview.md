# Getting Started with Continia Finance

Continia Finance simplifies finance-related tasks in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central with focused modules that integrate seamlessly into your workflow. Sign up easily, with no complex integrations required.

You can always get help with your subscription from your certified Continia partner. For more information, see [Resources for Help and Support](Help and Support.md).

## Get familiar with Continia Finance

The following pages provide a good overview of key Continia Finance features as well as insights into the latest developments:

* [Business Functionality](Business Functionality.md)
* [New and Planned](/Continia Finance/New and Planned/Overview.md)

As Continia Finance is built inside Business Central, the user interface will be familiar to existing users of Business Central. Some things will be unfamiliar to both new and experienced users though. To get help with anything you’re unsure about, check out one of the valuable resources listed below. Which resource to use depends on your situation:

* [Continia Docs](/) (this site) – the official Continia documentation site, which features a large number of helpful articles for beginners, IT professionals and anyone in between
* [Continia Learn](https://learn.continia.com/) – Continia's online learning platform with educational articles, videos, and exercises, completely free and accessible to anyone who's curious to know more about Continia Finance and other Continia solutions
* [Continia partners](Resellers and Partners/Overview.md) – the go-to point of contact for *end users*
* [Continia PartnerZone](https://partnerzone.continia.com/) – Continia’s dedicated learning portal *exclusively for partners*

All certified Continia partners are also official Business Central partners, and they’re all expert users of both Continia Finance and Business Central in general. To find out more, see [Resellers and Partners](Resellers and Partners/Overview.md).

> [!NOTE]
> If you're an end user and you can't find answers to your questions on Continia Docs or Continia Learn, please reach out to your existing Business Central partner for assistance. Access to Continia PartnerZone is exclusive to partners.

## Free trial using your own company

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The free-trial option is only available to users of Business Central online.

If you’re thinking about subscribing, you can try out Continia Finance for free for a 30-day trial period. To do this, simply go to [Microsoft AppSource](https://appsource.microsoft.com/) and search for **Continia Finance**. Choose the related link, and then select **Free trial** in the upper-left corner. This will install Continia Finance, which is then ready for activation and setup using assisted setup guides.

You must be assisted by your Continia partner when activating any version of Continia Finance, including trials. This ensures that everything is done correctly, and that you’re off to a good start with the app.

Once activated and set up, Continia Finance can be used to its full extent at absolutely no charge for up to 30 days. Toward the end of the free-trial period, you’ll be asked if you’d like to subscribe to Continia Finance in order to keep using it. If you decide to subscribe at any point during the trial period, you’re already up and running, and you can then continue your work and keep using the same data as in the trial period.

To learn more about trials and subscriptions, including information about sandbox environments, see [Continia Finance Trials and Subscriptions](@CF-39). If you have any questions, please reach out to your certified Continia partner. For more information, see [Where do I go if I have questions?](/continia-finance/getting-started/troubleshooting-and-faqs/faq#where-do-i-go-if-i-have-questions) and [Resources for Help and Support](Help and Support.md).

For any questions you might have, please reach out to your certified Continia partner. For more information, see [Resources for Help and Support](Help and Support.md).

## Looking for answers
If you have questions about functionality or are unsure about anything else in Continia Finance, you may be able to find the answers you’re looking for in the actual user interface of the app. Most fields have an associated tooltip that describes the field in question, and which usually also includes a **Learn more** link for further information.

If you’re unable to find what you’re looking for in the tooltips, please contact your Continia partner or check out the other resources mentioned above under [Get familiar with Continia Finance](/continia-finance/getting-started/overview#get-familiar-with-continia-finance).

## Ready to subscribe?
You can always subscribe to Continia Finance to get the full version of the app on a more permanent basis, with absolutely no limitations. If you like, you can subscribe right away and get started immediately, but you’re also very welcome to subscribe at any point during or directly following your free trial period. If you start off with a trial period, you’re already up and running at the time of subscription, and you can then continue working with the same data as in the trial period.

As mentioned above, you must be assisted by your Continia partner when activating any version of Continia Finance, in order to ensure that you get up and running properly.
