# Welcome to Continia Finance

Continia Finance offers a range of modules that aim to simplify and enhance your financial tasks within Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. The objective is to empower you by providing separate, focused modules that address specific finance-related needs, while seamlessly integrating into your existing workflow.

With Continia Finance, you can select individual modules that assist directly with various tasks, such as tracking associations, creating new assets, and generating open entries for General Ledger accounts. These separate add-ons help you tackle specific challenges, making your life easier.

For an overview of the available modules and their features, read the [Business Functionality Continia Finance](@CF-01) article.
