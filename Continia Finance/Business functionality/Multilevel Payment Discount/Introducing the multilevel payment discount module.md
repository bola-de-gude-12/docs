# Introducing the Multi-level Payment Discount Module

The Multiple Level Payment Discount module in Business Central allows you to create sales invoices with multi-level payment discounts. It helps set up payment terms with different levels of discounts. For example, 3% at 10 days, 2% at 20 days, and 1% at 30 days. When generating a sales invoice with these terms, the discounts are automatically applied based on the specified due dates.
