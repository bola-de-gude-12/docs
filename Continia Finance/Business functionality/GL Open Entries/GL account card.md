# The G/L Account Card

If you activated the G/L Open Entries module, the Continia Finance tab is accessible from the action bar on the G/L account card. It offers the following options:

* G/L Account Open Entries
* Ext. Detail Trial Balance
* Ext. G/L Total Balance
