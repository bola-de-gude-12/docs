# Introducing the Association module

The Associations module facilitates the creation of customer associations, allowing you to group any number of customers, or vendor associations by combining multiple vendors. With this feature, you are able to view all open entries that belong to an association, making it easier to assign payments.

The module has two components:

- You can merge any number of customers into a customer association or any number of vendors into a vendor association.
  ![Associations](/images/CFI/associations.png)
- You can link a customer with a vendor to form a customer/vendor link for clearing entries.
