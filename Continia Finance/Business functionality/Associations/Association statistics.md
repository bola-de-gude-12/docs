# Viewing Association Statistics

Association statistics provide valuable insights and data-driven perspectives into the performance and dynamics of the associations you have set up. By analyzing these statistics, you can gain a deeper understanding of how associations impact your organization's financial health, efficiency, and decision-making processes.

To view the statistics of an association:

1. On the **Association Card** or the **Association List**, navigate to the main menu and select **Statistics**. 
2. On the **Statistics** page, the top section displays general information about the association. Several fields provide clickable links to access more detailed information. For example, clicking the **Balance** field value opens the **Customer Ledger Entries** page to view the corresponding sales or purchase values and other association data.
3. The lower part of the statistics page lists the corresponding sales values (or purchase values) and other association data.
