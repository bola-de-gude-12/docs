# Open Customer Entries

This article describes the **Open Customer Entries** report in Continia Finance, which shows customer-related receivables or payables that have yet to be cleared.

To access this report, choose the {{search}} icon, enter **Open Customer Entries**, and then choose the related link.

To navigate to the first, last, previous, or next sets of customer entries, use the corresponding actions under the **Home** action menu. Selecting **Home** > **Card** opens the related customer card, and selecting **Home** > **Ledger Entries** open the related **Customer Ledger Entries** page.

Switch to the **Reports** action menu to print one of the customer reports, or to send the balance confirmation via email. Note that you have to configure the fields under the **Balance Confirmation Email** section on the **Reporting** FastTab of the **Extended Financial Reports Setup** page before you can send balance confirmations via email.

>[!NOTE]
>The **Reports** dropdown menu includes the same actions as the **Reports** action menu, as well as the **Customer - Open Entries** action. Select this to configure and print or export the related report.

On the **Customer** FastTab, under the **Name** section, you can see the selected customer's contact details – such as number, name, address, and post code.

<a href="/images/CFI/open-customer-entries-top.png" target="_blank"><img src="/images/CFI/open-customer-entries-top.png" alt="Open Customer Entries - top section"></a>

Under the **Payment** section, you find the date and amount of the selected customer's last payment, as well as information related to the payment terms and payment method. Finally, the **Statistics** section shows the open and due amounts.

The **Open Customer Ledger Entries** table shows the ledger entries related to the selected customer, including their association number (if applicable).

<a href="/images/CFI/open-customer-entries-bottom.png" target="_blank"><img src="/images/CFI/open-customer-entries-bottom.png" alt="Open Customer Entries - bottom section"></a>

Select **Manage** > **Find Entries** to search for entries and documents based on the document number, external document number, or posting date of the selected entry.

Select **Entry** and the actions below are shown.

* Reminder/Fin. Charge Entries: Shows reminders and finance charge entries related to the selected customer.
* Applied Entries: Shows the ledger entries applied to the record.
* Dimensions: View or edit dimensions – such as area, project, or department – that you can assign to sales and purchase document to distribute costs and analyze transaction history.
* Detailed Ledger Entries: Shows a summary of all the posted entries and adjustments related to a specific customer ledger entry.
* Multi-Level Pmt. Discount: View or edit the conditions for the multi-level payment discounts linked to this entry.

Select **Functions** and the actions below are shown.

* Apply Entries: Select one or more entries to apply to this record, so the related posted documents are closed as paid or refunded.
* Unapply Entries: Removes one or more previously applied entries.
* Reverse Transaction: Reverses a customer ledger entry.
