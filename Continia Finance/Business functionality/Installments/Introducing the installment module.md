# Introducing the Installment Module

The Installment module in Continia Finance simplifies the management of scheduled payments. It allows you to create, track, and manage payments over specific periods. With this setup, you can divide transactions into smaller payments and assign them to their respective due dates. You can designate customers or vendors for installment payments using a template or apply an installment payment template when posting a document.
