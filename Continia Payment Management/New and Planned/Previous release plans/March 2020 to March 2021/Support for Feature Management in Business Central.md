#Support for Feature Management in Business Central

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |

## Feature details

Going forward, certain minor features will be made available in Business Central before they're released as part of our major releases, in accordance with the [Feature Management](/Continia Payment Management/Development and Administration/Feature Management.md) program. If you want to activate such new features in your solution before the next major release, you must actively decide to do so.
