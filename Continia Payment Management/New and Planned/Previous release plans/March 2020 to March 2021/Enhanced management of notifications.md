#Enhanced Management of Notifications

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Enhanced management of notifications | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2020 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2020 | - |

## Feature details
You can now send notifications using job queues, and the language of the email template that's used for sending notifications can be selected automatically. In case of problems identified during the sending of email notifications, you can check the overview of failed email notifications, which enables you to quickly correct the issues and have the notifications sent.
