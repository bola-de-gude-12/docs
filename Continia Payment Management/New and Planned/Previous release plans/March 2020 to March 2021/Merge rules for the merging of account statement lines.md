#Merge Rules for the Merging of Account Statement Lines

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Merge rules for the merging of account statement  lines | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2020 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2020 | - |

## Feature details
You can now merge account statement lines using the predefined merge rules, either automatically when loading the account statement or manually in the reconciliation journal. Merging payment lines may be useful if you need to combine payments relating to credit card terminals or similar.
