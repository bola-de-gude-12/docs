#Customer Reconciliation Rules to Identify Customers

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Customer reconciliation rules to identify customers | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2020 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2020 | - |

## Feature details
Using customer reconciliation rules, you can specify one or more search rules to identify the customer in a bank account entry. If the search rule is matched, the identified customer's customer number will then be added to the bank account reconciliation line, after which settlement can be performed.
