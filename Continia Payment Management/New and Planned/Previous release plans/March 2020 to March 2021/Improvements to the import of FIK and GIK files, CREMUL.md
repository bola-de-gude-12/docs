#Improvements to the Import of FIK and GIK Files

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Improvements to the import of FIK and GIK files | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2020 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2020 | - |

## Feature details

New search parameters have been added to the payment journal, enabling Continia Payment Management to identify customers based on payment notifications. It does this by searching for each individual customer in the fields **Name** and **Search name**.

As a user, you get better assistance when working with cash receipt journal lines in the cash receipt journal: You're now notified if the same cash receipt journal lines have been loaded into the cash receipt journal more than once. Also, a new feature has been added to the journal to handle such lines, preventing them from being loaded again later. 

Details about any potential amount differences, for example in relation to merged payment lines, are provided on the relevant payment line.

Additional improvements have been made to the management of notifications, ensuring that address details identified for each payment are now included in the corresponding notification. In addition, notifications appear in a fact box on the right side of the screen.
