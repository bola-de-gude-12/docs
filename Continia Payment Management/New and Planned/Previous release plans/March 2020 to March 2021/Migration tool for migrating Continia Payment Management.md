#Migration Tool for Migrating Continia Payment Management

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Migration tool for migrating Continia Payment  Management | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2020 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2020 | - |

## Feature details
If you wish to upgrade Continia Payment Management *FOB version* to Continia Payment Management *App version* for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central 2019 release wave 2 (BC15) or later, Continia now provides a migration tool that enables you to do this quickly and easily. The migration tool ensures that all data and setup details are maintained in the new solution, thereby getting you up and running in Business Central quickly and seamlessly.

The migration tool is adapted to each individual Payment Management version, so you'll need a version of the tool that's suitable for your particular upgrade. The migration tool is now available for download at Continia [PartnerZone](https://partnerzone.continia.com/downloads/) - put a tick in solution **Payment Management** and a tick in the tag **Misc** and you will find the migration tool.

For guidance on migrating from Business Central on-premises to the cloud, refer to the [Migrating Business Central On-Premises to Cloud](@PM-192) article.

For details on migrating from FOB to App, refer to the [Migrating from FOB to App](@PM-53) article.
