#Localization and release of Payment Management for the Norwegian Business Central market (NO)

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Localization and release of Payment Management for the Norwegian Business Central market (NO) | {{checkmark}} Apr 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Apr 1, 2021 | - |

##Feature

To bring the capabilities of Payment Management to even more Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central users, Payment Management has now been localized and released for the Norwegian Business Central market (NO).

This release includes

- Handling of KID payments and import of KID cash receipts.
- Direct communication with DNB.
- All captions and tooltips will be translated into Norwegian. 

The NO localization is available from Payment Management version 2.1.0.0
