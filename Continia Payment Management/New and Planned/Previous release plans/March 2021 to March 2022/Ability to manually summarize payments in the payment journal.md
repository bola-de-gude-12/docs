#Ability to manually summarize payments in the payment journal

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Ability to manually summarize payments in the  payment journal | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2021 | - |

##Feature details

This feature contains improvements for the action of merging payments by offering a larger selection of parameters to define the merge. With the new improved functionality it will also be possible to execute the merge of payments manually, after the payment lines have been created in the payment journal. For more information, see [Summarizing payments](@PM-186).

This feature is available from Payment Management version 3.0.0.0.
