#Improvements to the Payment Management migration tool

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Improvements to the Payment Management migration  tool | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2021 | - |

## Business value

Improvements to the documentation of the migration tool so it becomes even more intuitive and easy for partners to use in the migration of Payment Management .fob solution to the app solution. 

## Feature details

Improvements to documentation of the migration tool to simplify the upgrading of Continia Payment Management from NAV/BC14 (.fob) to Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central 2021 release wave 1 (BC18).

The improved migration tool is available from Payment Management version 3.0.0.0.
