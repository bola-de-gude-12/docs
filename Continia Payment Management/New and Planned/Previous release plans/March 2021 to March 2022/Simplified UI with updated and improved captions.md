#Simplified UI with updated and improved captions

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Simplified UI with updated and improved captions | {{checkmark}} Mar 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Apr 1, 2021 |-|

##Details

The UI updates are part of Payment Management version 2.0.0.0.

We are constantly working on making Payment Management even more user-friendly and intuitive. In that regard, Payment Management has through a thorough review of the user interface, for several page layouts, captions, and tooltips. 

Especially the Payment Management Setup page has been made simpler, and several settings have been moved to separate setup pages, which can be accessed from the action bar of the Payment Management setup page.

All texts such as descriptions, messages, captions, and tooltips have been examined with the intention to make the solution more intuitive and easily understood.

For you, this means that you might have to navigate to another page than normal to find a specific setting and that actions and boxes might have other captions than what you are used to seeing.

We are aware that this can be challenging for a short amount of time, however, we are confident this will make the overall experience of working with Payment Management even greater.

This is a continuous process, and we expect to have more of these improvements coming in the next year.
