#Localization and release of Payment Management for the Irish Business Central market (IE)

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Localization and release of Payment Management for the Irish Business Central market (IE) | {{checkmark}} Mar 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Apr 1, 2021 | - |

##Details

To bring the capabilities of Payment Management to even more Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central users, Payment Management has now been localized and released for the Irish Business Central market (IE).

The IE localization is available from Payment Management version 2.0.0.0
