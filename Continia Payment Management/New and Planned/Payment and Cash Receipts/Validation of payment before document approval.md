# Validation of payment before document approval

| Feature | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Validation of payment before document approval | {{checkmark}} Oct 2023 | - |



## Business value

This feature in the Payment Management Setup puts you in control of your validation process. With the ability to toggle dynamic validation on or off, you can decide when validation will be executed before approvals are granted.


## Feature details

Simply navigate to the Payment Setup and enable or disable dynamic validation as needed.

To learn more about this payment validation feature, see the [Setting up purchase documents](@PM-177#to-manage-purchase-document-validation) article.
