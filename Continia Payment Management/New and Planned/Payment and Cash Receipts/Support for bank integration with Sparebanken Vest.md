# Support for Bank Integration with Sparebanken Vest



| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------: | :-------------------------: | :------------------------: |
| Support for bank integration with Sparebanken Vest using the digital service TietoEVRY. | {{checkmark}} Mar 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |

## Feature details

As a customer of Sparebanken Vest, you can send and retrieve bank files between Sparebanken Vest and Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central using the Payment Management Direct Communication module. 

For more information about how to onboard Sparebanken Vest with Payment Management, see [Setting up Sparebanken Vest to use Direct Communication](@PM-219).

This feature is available from Payment Management version 4.0.
