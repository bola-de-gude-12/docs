# Support for Direct Communication with Norwegian banks

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Support for direct communication with Norwegian banks | - | {{checkmark}} Jan 2023 | - |



## Business value

To integrate with more banks in Norway, Payment Management offers support for direct communication through TietoEvry for [Sparebanken Møre](@PM-314) and [Sparebanken Sør](@PM-315). 

## Feature details

Sparebanken Møre and Sparebanken Sør are Norwegian savings banks for which Payment Management collaborates with the digital service Tietoevry to send bank data between the banks and Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central.

For more information on how to set up direct communication with Sparebanken Møre, see the [Sparebanken Møre](@PM-314) article.

For more information on how to set up direct communication with Sparebanken Sør, see the [Sparebanken Sør](@PM-315) article.
