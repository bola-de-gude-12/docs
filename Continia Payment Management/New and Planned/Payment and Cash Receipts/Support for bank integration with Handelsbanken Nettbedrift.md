# Support for Bank Integration with Handelsbanken Nettbedrift



| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------: | :-------------------------: | :------------------------: |
| Support for bank integration with Handelsbanken Nettbedrift using the digital service TietoEVRY. | {{checkmark}} Mar 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |



## Feature details

As a customer of Handelsbanken Nettbedrift, you can send and retrieve bank files between Handelsbanken Nettbedrift and Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central using the Payment Management Direct Communication module. 

This feature is available from Payment Management version 4.0.

For more information about how to onboard Sparebanken Vest with Payment Management, see [Onboarding Handelsbanken Nettbedrift to use Direct Communication](@PM-221).
