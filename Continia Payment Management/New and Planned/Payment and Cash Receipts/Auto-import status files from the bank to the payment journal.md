# Auto-import Status Files From the Bank to the Payment Journal

| Feature | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Auto-import status files from the bank to the  payment journal | {{checkmark}} Dec 2022 | - |

## Business value

Automation of the payment process is one of our focus areas within Payment Management. We aim to help users with important and valuable tasks and reduce the time spent on trivial yet important tasks. This will free up time to do other tasks on a busy workday.

## Feature details

To automate and improve the payment flow, Payment Management will support the automatic import of status files from the bank and into the payment journal, ensuring real-time updates of the status of the payment lines. This service ensures that the status of the payments in the payment journal is always up to date, making it possible to quickly respond to payments that require further attention or management. 

For more information, see the [Using the Payment Status](@PM-89) article.
