# Automatic Posting of Payments in the Payment Journal


| Feature | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Automatic posting of payments in the payment journal | {{checkmark}} April 2024 | - |



## Business value

Automation of the payment process is one of our focus areas within Payment Management. We aim to help users with important and valuable tasks and reduce the time spent on trivial, yet important tasks. This will free up time to do other tasks in a busy workday.

## Feature details

To automate and improve the payment flow, Payment Management will support the automatic posting of payments in the payment journal, based on the status of the payment lines. This service will help ensure that vendor payments are processed and issued in the payment journal.

See the [Suggesting and processing payments](@PM-90#to-post-payments-automatically) article for more information.
