# Support for Bank Integration with Barclays



| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------: | :-------------------------: | :------------------------: |
| Support for bank integration with Barclays | {{checkmark}} Mar 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |



## Feature details

As a customer Barclays you can send and retrieve bank files between Barclays and Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central using the Payment Management Direct Communication service. 

This feature is available from Payment Management version 4.0.

> [!IMPORTANT]
>
> Before setting up the connection with Barclays Bank, please contact us first.
