# New Payment Approval Management Feature

| Feature | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| New Payment Approval Management feature | {{checkmark}} Oct 2022 | - |



## Business value

Approval of payments is a critical task for most businesses, and with this feature, we will support approval workflows that meet the challenges of setting up more advanced payment approval workflows in Business Central. 


## Feature details

With the Payment Approval module, you can set up a secure and safe approval process for approving vendor, employee, and customer payments. You can set up one approval workflow per payment journal and specify whether the lines must be approved individually or if the entire batch must be approved as one. You can only set up and use Payment Approval on Payment Management-enabled payment journals.

To learn more about the Payment Approval feature, see the [Overview of Setting up Payment Approval](@PM-257) article.
