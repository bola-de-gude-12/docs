# Support for Bank Integration with the SpareBank 1 Alliance

| Support for bank integration with the SpareBank 1 alliance using the digital service TietoEVRY. | {{checkmark}} Mar 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |


## Feature details

SpareBank 1 is an alliance consisting of 15 independent banks across Norway that collaborate on a common platform and brand. As a customer of one of the banks in this collaboration you can send and retrieve bank files between SpareBank 1 and Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central using the Payment Management Direct Communication service. SpareBank 1 uses the digital service TietoEVRY to establish the bank integration from Business Central to SpareBank 1's online bank.

For more information about how to onboard a SpareBank 1 bank with Payment Management, see [Onboarding SpareBank 1 with direct communication](@PM-213).

This feature is available from Payment Management version 4.0.
