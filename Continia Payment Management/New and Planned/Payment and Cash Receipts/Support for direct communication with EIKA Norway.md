# Support for Direct Communication with Eika, Norway

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Support for direct communication with Eika, Norway | {{checkmark}} | {{checkmark}} | - |

## Business value

As more companies in Norway have seen the benefits of using Payment Management, we now add support for direct communication with all Eika banks in Norway.

## Feature details

This integration increases our support for Norwegian banks significantly, as we now support all major banks in Norway. The integration is made through Tietoevry and covers both payments and account statements.

For supported banks, see the [Supported banks in Norway](@PM-111) article. 

For more information how to set up communication, see the [Eika Alliance](@PM-377) article.
