# End-user Installation and setup of Payment Management

| Feature | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| End-user Installation of Payment Management | {{checkmark}} Oct 2022 | - |

## Business Value

When new customers can install and try out Payment Management without the assistance of a Continia Partner, they have the freedom to try it out and get a good feel of the benefits before they decide to include a partner. The installation and setup of Payment Management have also been improved for the purpose of helping new customers quickly get started with the basics. 




## Feature Details

With this feature, customers can now install Continia Payment Management without the assistance of a Continia Partner. Customers can run Payment Management in a 30-day trial period after which they'll have to contact a partner to start a subscription.
