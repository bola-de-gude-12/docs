# Ability to Share Certificates Across Companies

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Ability to share certificates across companies | {{checkmark}} Mar 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |

## Business value

This feature will be useful for businesses that have multiple companies set up in Business Central that use the same bank certificates. This could be a bank certificate which is needed by two companies that have Payment Management activated, thus instead of creating the bank certificate in one company and then having to export it and import it again to the other company, it will be possible for both companies to use the same certificate right away when the certificate has been created. 

## Feature details

The feature makes it possible to create bank certificates for direct bank communication in Business Central that can be shared between companies within Business Central.

For more information, see [Manage Tokens and Certificates](@PM-175)
