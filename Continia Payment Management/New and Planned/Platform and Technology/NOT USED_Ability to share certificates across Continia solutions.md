# Ability to share certificates across Continia solutions

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Ability to share certificates across Continia solutions | Mar 1, 2022 | Apr 1, 2022 | - |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Unfortunately, it hasn't been possible to complete this feature in time for it to be included in Continia Payment Management 2021 R2. The feature will be included in Payment Management 2022 R1 instead.

## Business value

This feature will be useful for businesses that have several Continia solutions installed, which needs to use the same bank certificates. This could be a bank certificate which is needed both by Continia Collection Management and Continia Payment Management, thus instead of creating the bank certificate for one solution and then having to export it and import it again to another solution, it will be possible for both solutions to use the same certificate right away when the certificate has been created. 

## Feature details

The feature makes it possible to create bank certificates for direct bank communication in Business Central that can be shared between Continia solutions within Business Central.
