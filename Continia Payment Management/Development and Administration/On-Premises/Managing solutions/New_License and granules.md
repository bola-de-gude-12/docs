# License and Granules for Continia Payment Management

Payment Management consists of multiple functional areas, however, not all customers might request the same functionality. To facilitate this, Payment Management contains granules that each represent a small area of functionality. This makes it possible for each customer to purchase the exact functionality of Payment Management that brings value to their company.

Payment Management on-premises offers the following granules:

| Granule | Granule Name |
| --- | --- |
| 6.016.855 | Continia Payment Management Essential |
| 6.016.860 | Continia Payment Management Direct Communication |
| 6.016.865 | Continia Payment Management IBAN Lookup |
| 6.016.870 | Continia Payment Management Statement Intelligence |
| 6.192.800 | Continia Payment Management Payment Approval |
| 6.192.805 | Continia Payment Management Direct Debit |
| 6.192.810 | Continia Payment Management Service Provider Import |

> [!IMPORTANT]
>
> The Essential module is required in order to use Payment Management. Also, the billing of Payment Management depends on how many modules the customer has in the license.
>
