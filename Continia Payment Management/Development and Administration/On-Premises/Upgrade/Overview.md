# Upgrading to the Latest Version

This section provides details on how to upgrade to the latest version of Payment Management in the *on-premises* version of Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central (including guidelines on how to migrate from on-premises to the cloud). You can find guidelines for the online version of Business Central [here](@PM-19).  

To upgrade Payment Management for *app* versions of Business Central, you must use the specially developed Continia PowerShell script that automatically upgrades the app and all dependencies. For more information, see [Upgrading Payment Management for app versions of Business Central](@PM-17).

If your organization is looking to move from an on-premises deployment to a cloud environment, you can easily do so by following the steps described on Microsoft Docs [Migrating On-Premises Data to Business Central Online](https://docs.microsoft.com/da-dk/dynamics365/business-central/dev-itpro/administration/migrate-data).

>[!IMPORTANT]
>After any upgrade (for both app and FOB versions of Business Central), you must always download and use a new and updated customer license file from Microsoft. Note that the granules and modules for PM FOB and PM App belong to different series.

>[!TIP]
>You can download the Payment Management installation packages and upgrade files on the [Continia Partnerzone](https://partnerzone.continia.com/downloads/)
