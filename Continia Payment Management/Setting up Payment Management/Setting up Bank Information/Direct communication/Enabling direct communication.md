# Enabling the Direct Communication Module

Before setting up your bank accounts for direct communication, you must enable the Direct Communication module in Continia Solution Management. 

To successfully enable the Direct Communication Module, you must meet specific requirements:

- Payment Management Administrator rights and credentials. 
- Your company must be registered as a production company. Testing or demo environments such as test companies or sandboxes can't be used for this purpose.
- For the on-premises version of Business Central, ask your Payment Management partner to add the license granule "6.016.860 - Continia Payment Management Direct Communication" to your license file. For more information, see [Managing Licenses](@PM-176). 

To enable Direct Communication:

1. In Business Central, select the icon ![Search for page or report](/images/search_small.png), enter **Continia Solution Management**, and select the related link.

1. In the Continia solutions overview, select **Payment Management**.

1. On the action bar, select **Manage** > **Manage Modules** and follow the instructions provided in the assisted setup guide to activate the module.

   > [!NOTE]
   >
   > Activating additional modules in Payment Management will result in charges being added to your next invoice.
