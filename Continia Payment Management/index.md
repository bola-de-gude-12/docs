# Welcome to Continia Payment Management

Payment Management is an end-to-end solution for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central that enables you to streamline and automate vendor payment and bank account reconciliation processes. 

{{include "end-of-sale-pm"}} 

With access to a wide range of payment methods, complete and seamless integration between Business Central and your bank, and automatic generation of cash receipt journal lines and reconciliation suggestions, you can easily stay on top of your payment processes and eliminate the risk of error – all in one place.
