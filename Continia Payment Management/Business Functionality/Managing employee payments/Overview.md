# Managing Employee Payments

Payment Management offers a variety of ways for you to manage your employee payments. The most important of these are described in the articles listed in the table below.

| To                                                           | See                                                 |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Create payment suggestions, view additional payment details, and send/export or post payments | [Suggesting and Processing Payments](@PM-363)       |
| Learn how to interpret, update, or reset the statuses of payment lines | [Using the Payment Status](@PM-89)                  |
| Define balance accounts directly on payment lines or update balance accounts for multiple payment lines at once | [Applying Balance Account to Payment Lines](@PM-91) |
| Summarize payment lines in the payment journal automatically or manually | [Summarizing Payments](@PM-201)                     |
| Send, adjust, or restore email notifications, and manage unsent notifications | [Managing Payment Notifications](@PM-197)           |
| Learn about the payment methods supported by Payment Management | [Payment Methods](@PM-248)                          |
