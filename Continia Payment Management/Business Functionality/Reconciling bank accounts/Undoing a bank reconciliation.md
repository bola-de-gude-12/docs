# Undoing Bank Account Reconciliation

Occasionally, you may want to undo a reconciliation, for example, because you must fix discrepancies, change the month-end bank balance, or fix a mistake. Payment Management lets you undo a reconciliation posted from the bank account card. 

1. From the Role Center, from the action menu, select **Bank Accounts.**
2. Open the relevant Bank Account Card, select **Bank Account** > **Statements**, and then select the reconciliation. 
3. Go to the line in the statement that you want to delete and select **Undo**. This will reopen the reconciliation where you select **Matching** > **Match Automatically** to perform a new reconciliation. 
4. After making the appropriate changes, you must post the reconciliation again.
