# Posting Open Bank Account Ledger Entries

Before starting a new bank account reconciliation, we recommend that all open bank account ledger entries are posted. This is to keep a clean and clear from 



before the start-up date be closed so that they do not make noise when the bank account reconciliation is started.

In Payment Management bank account reconciliation module, a proposal is made that is based on the open bank account entries - a "bank statement" is generated from a date filter. It allows you to close bank records up to the date the bank account reconciliation takes effect. Once the old bank records are closed, the system will in future search and match on relevant bank records using Payment Management's search principles.

How to post open records
Select the Search for Page or Report icon or press Alt + Q, enter Bank Account Reconciliation, and then select the related link.
Create a new poll if this has not already been done.
Select Actions and then Function and Suggest lines.
Then fill in the relevant start and end date.
A number of bank account statement lines are now generated that match the open bank account entries.
Select Posting and then Post. You can read more about Business Central's standard bank account reconciliation on the Microsoft website.
