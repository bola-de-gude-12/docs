# Payment Management Danish Documentation

Note that there are two different versions of Payment Management: *Payment Management* (compatible with Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central 2019 wave 1 (BC14) and older) and *Payment Management 365* (compatible with Dynamics 365 Business Central 2019 wave 2 (BC15) and later). The content of this entire section is based on Payment Management 365, unless otherwise specified.

Also, because Continia Docs is currently undergoing a major overhaul, the content that's available in English differs from the Danish content. For the time being, installation guides and user guides are only available on the Danish version of Docs. You can find the Danish content [here (Payment Management 365 for Business Central)](https://docs.continia.com/da-dk/continia-payment-management) and [here (Payment Management for NAV and BC14)](https://docs.continia.com/da-dk/continia-payment-management-nav-bc14).
