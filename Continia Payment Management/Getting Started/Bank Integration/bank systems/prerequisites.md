## Prerequisites for Bizcuit signup

The following information is needed when signing up to Bizcuit:

- Your private bank account. In order to create a business account at Bizcuit you need to use your private bank account to identify yourself. This bank account can be from any bank that offers [iDIN](#idin).
- Your private bank card identifier.
- Information about the company bank account that should be linked to Bizcuit.
- Your company bank card identifier.
- Your Chamber of Commerce registration number. 



## Prerequisites - obtain certificate information from the bank

Setting up direct communication with Payment Management requires an agreement with your bank. When establishing an agreement with the bank you will be provided with user information that is required to create a certificate for bank communication in Business Central.

> [!NOTE]
>
> Not all banks support direct communication. Contact your bank to find out if they offers direct bank communication.
