# General FAQ
In this section, you can find answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about Continia Payment Management. The answers provide information on anything from availability to zero-cost trials and also include multiple links to other sites with more information.

## Is Payment Management available in my country?
Payment Management is available in numerous countries and languages. You can find the complete lists [here](/Continia Payment Management/Development and Administration/Countries and Languages.md).

## How do I get Payment Management?
Continia works through a network of specialized partners who are also Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central experts. Contact your Continia partner to get started quickly.

See also: [Finding a reseller of Continia Payment Management](/Continia Payment Management/Getting Started/Resellers and Partners/Find a Reseller.md).

You can also start with a free 30-day trial or set up a sandbox environment. For more information, see [Continia Payment Management Trials and Subscriptions](/Continia Payment Management/Getting Started/Trials and Subscriptions.md).

## Where do I go if I have questions?

If you have any questions about Payment Management that you can't find answers to in this documentation, you’re more than welcome to ask your Continia partner.

See [Resources for Help and Support](/Continia Payment Management/Getting Started/Help and Support.md) and [Resellers and Partners](/Continia Payment Management/Getting Started/Resellers and Partners/Overview.md) for more information.

## What does it cost to use Payment Management?

The cost of using Payment Management for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central online depends on how many modules you choose and how many posted transaction lines you send to and receive from your bank. For Business Central on-premises, the cost is based on other parameters, which you can find in the link below.

You can find detailed information on pricing for both online and on-premises usage [here](https://www.continia.com/pricing/). For a detailed price list, [view the price sheet](<https://partnerzone.blob.core.windows.net/documents/info/PriceList/January 2024/From Jan 2024/PM/PM Cloud - prisark EURO_JAN2024.pdf>).
