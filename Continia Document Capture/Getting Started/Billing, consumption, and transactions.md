# Billing, Consumption, and Transactions

This article provides information regarding billing and consumption for Continia Document Capture.

## Billing and Pricing

All billing related to Continia solutions is dealt with by your partner. For detailed information on pricing, see [Continia's Pricing page](https://www.continia.com/pricing/).

## Consumption

Continia doesn't charge a monthly or yearly fee for the use of Document Capture. Therefore, you pay according to your usage per calendar month.

All registered documents count toward your consumption, be them PDF or XML documents. However, files attached via drag and drop – as well as PDFs that were OCR-processed but not registered – don't count toward your consumption.

What's more, consumption counts per document, not per page. Therefore, both a single-page document and a five-page document count as single documents.

Consumption is tracked per Business Central tenant. There are no extra fees related to additional companies, bank accounts, production environments, Business Central users, or usage of the Continia Delivery Network (CDN).

Finally, consumption-related data is synchronized between Document Capture and Continia Online every 15 minutes. If no usage data is received for 14 days, Document Capture is disabled.

## Transactions

To check your transactions:

1. Choose the {{search}} icon, enter **Continia Solution Management**, and then select the related link.
2. In the list of installed Continia solutions, select the solution for which you want to see the transactions.
3. On the action bar, click **Related** > **Logs** > **Show Transactions**.
4. You can then see a list of transactions, including the related names of the companies, descriptions, dates and times when the documents were sent, and the IDs of the users who sent them.

To share usages with someone else, select **Actions** > **Send Usages** on the action bar.
