# Getting started with Document Capture

Document Capture is an end-to-end solution for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central that enables you to import, OCR-process, register, approve, and archive invoices and other business documents. It’s easy to sign up and get started, and there’s no need for complex integrations, as Document Capture is already fully integrated into Business Central.

You can get help with your subscription from your certified Continia partner. For more information, see [Where do I go if I have questions?](@DC-225#where-do-i-go-if-i-have-questions) and [Resources for help and support](@DC-14).

## Extended Role Centers
Document Capture is an add-on that enhances the user interface and functionality of Business Central. It does so by extending some of the standard Role Centers in Business Central, adding a section that features a number of useful cues and action tiles.

This section, **Continia Document Capture Activities**, is divided into several cue groups – each of which contains multiple relevant cues or action tiles. The various cues relate to the OCR-processing, import, registration, and approval of invoices and credit memos, while the action tiles perform different tasks and operations when selected.

The following standard Business Central Role Centers have been extended by default with this section:

* **Accountant**
* **Accounting Manager**
* **Accounts Payable Coordinator**
* **Bookkeeper**
* **Business Manager**

> [!NOTE]
> In addition to the above Role Centers, Continia partners can extend any additional Business Central Role Center with the **Continia Document Capture Activities** section as well. This is done using the page **6085573 CDC Doc. Capture Activities**. If this is relevant to you, reach out to [your Continia partner](@DC-15).

## Free trial using your own company

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The free-trial option is only available to users of Business Central online.

You can try out Document Capture for free for a 30-day trial period. To do this, go to [Microsoft AppSource](https://appsource.microsoft.com/) and search for **Continia Document Capture**. Select the related link, and then click **Free trial** in the upper-left corner. This installs Document Capture, which is then ready for activation and setup using assisted setup guides.

You must be assisted by your Continia partner when activating any version of Document Capture, including trials. This ensures that everything is done correctly, and that you’re off to a good start with the app.

Once activated and set up, Document Capture can be used to its full extent at no charge for up to 30 days or until the maximum number of free-trial documents have been registered. Toward the end of the free-trial period, you’re asked if you would like to subscribe to Document Capture to keep using it. If you decide to subscribe at any point during the trial period, you’re already up and running – and you can then continue your work and keep using the same data as in the trial period.

To learn more about trials and subscriptions, including information about sandbox environments, see [Continia Document Capture trials and subscriptions](@DC-168). If you have any questions, reach out to your certified Continia partner. For more information, see [Where do I go if I have questions?](@DC-225#where-do-i-go-if-i-have-questions) and [Resources for help and support](@DC-14).

## Looking for answers
If you have questions about functionality or are unsure about anything else in Document Capture, you may be able to find the answers you’re looking for in the actual user interface of the app. Most fields have an associated tooltip that describes the field in question – and often includes a **Learn more** link for further details.

If you’re unable to find what you’re looking for in the tooltips, contact your Continia partner or check out the other resources mentioned above under [Get familiar with Document Capture](/continia-document-capture/getting-started/overview#get-familiar-with-document-capture).

## Ready to subscribe?
You can subscribe to Document Capture to get the full version of the app on a more permanent basis. You can subscribe right away and get started immediately, but you’re also very welcome to subscribe at any point during or directly following your free trial period.

As mentioned above, you must be assisted by your Continia partner when activating any version of Document Capture, in order to ensure that you get up and running properly.
