# Managing Electronic Documents in the United States
As a customer in the United States, you can import electronic documents into Continia Document Capture by email (as attached files).

The United States doesn't yet have a fixed strategi for adopting the Peppol eDelivery Network. Considering the wide adoption of Peppol in Europe and beyond, Continia hopes that the United States too will join Peppol to establish a unified and frictionless exchange of electronic documents. Currently, the United States is evaluating Peppol as a country-wide standard for the future.

However, as an alternative to Peppol, you can still import electronic documents as email attachments. Most widespread formats are supported, but not all. For a list of supported formats, see [Supported Formats](Supported Formats.md).
