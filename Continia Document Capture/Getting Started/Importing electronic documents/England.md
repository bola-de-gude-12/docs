# Importing Electronic Documents in England
As a customer in England, you can import electronic documents into Continia Document Capture either by email (as attached files) or via the Peppol eDelivery Network.

Document Capture connects you directly to the Peppol eDelivery Network, thereby eliminating the need to sign up with any third parties for integration. Once connected, you can start receiving electronic documents from vendors in England and other countries. To learn more about how to connect to the Peppol eDelivery Network, see [Setting up the Continia Delivery Network](/Continia Document Capture/Setting up Document Capture/Setting up general business functionality/Setting up the Continia Delivery Network.md).

There's currently no standardization or consensus on the use of Peppol in England. However, the National Health Service (NHS) in England has decided to use Peppol as a document delivery method, and it's being adopted as a messaging standard when dealing with the NHS.

Document Capture supports the Peppol BIS 3 format when it comes to processing billing documents such as invoices and credit memos.

You can also receive and import electronic documents from vendors in other countries. Most widespread formats are supported, but not all. For a list of supported formats, see [Supported Formats](Supported Formats.md).
