# Managing electronic documents
Exchanging electronic documents directly with Continia Document Capture is a straightforward way to optimize your business. More and more public authorities around the world are mandating electronic exchange of documents, and this is also becoming increasingly popular among business-to-business organizations – which is why it's an essential part of Document Capture.

Your options for exchanging electronic documents with Document Capture depend on which types of electronic documents and files you're exchanging. You can exchange files by connecting to the Continia Delivery Network. Additionally, you can send files to an email address monitored by Document Capture.

The Continia Delivery Network is a service that integrates with the Peppol eDelivery Network and the Danish NemHandel network, thereby enabling you to send and receive electronic documents directly in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. Continia is a certified Peppol access point, which eliminates the need for any third-party vendors to integrate your company into the Peppol eDelivery Network. When you sign up to the Continia Delivery Network, you're automatically set up as a recipient in the global Peppol eDelivery Network. To learn more about how to connect with the Peppol eDelivery Network or the NemHandel network, see [Setting up the Continia Delivery Network](/Continia Document Capture/Setting up Document Capture/Setting up general business functionality/Setting up the Continia Delivery Network.md).  

When using an email address monitored by Document Capture, you can import electronic documents in many formats. Most widespread formats are supported. For a list of supported formats, see [Supported formats](Supported Formats.md).
