# Managing Electronic Documents in  Finland
As a customer in Finland, you can import electronic documents into Continia Document Capture either by email (as attached files) or via the Peppol eDelivery Network.

Document Capture connects you directly to the Peppol eDelivery Network, thereby eliminating the need to sign up with any third parties for integration. Once connected, you can start receiving electronic documents from vendors in Finland and other countries. To learn more about how to connect to the Peppol eDelivery Network, see [Setting up the Continia Delivery Network](/Continia Document Capture/Setting up Document Capture/Setting up general business functionality/Setting up the Continia Delivery Network.md).

The Finvoice and TEAPPSXML formats have been widely adopted by both businesses and public authorities in Finland. Out of these two formats, Document Capture currently supports Finvoice but not TEAPPSXML. However, the latter will most likely be supported in the future.

You can also receive and import electronic documents from vendors in other countries. Most widespread formats are supported, but not all. For a list of supported formats, see [Supported Formats](Supported Formats.md).
