# Importing electronic documents in the Netherlands

As a customer in the Netherlands, you can import electronic documents into Continia Document Capture either by email (as attached files) or via the Peppol eDelivery Network.

To promote the general adoption of Peppol, the entire central government and most of the decentral government in the Netherlands strongly advise to use the Peppol eDelivery Network. Document Capture supports the Peppol BIS 3 format, which is the latest standard.

You can also receive and import electronic documents from vendors in other countries. Most widespread formats are supported, but not all. For a list of supported formats, see [Supported electronic document formats](@DC-55).
