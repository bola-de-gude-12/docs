# Modules for Continia Document Capture

Continia Document Capture comes with a number of modules (also known as granules) that each represent an area of functionality which you may or may not need, depending on your geographical location and your organizational needs and wants. For a list of these and all additional Continia modules, see [ISV modules to add to NAV/Business Central license](https://partnerzone.continia.com/partnerzone/my-continia/isv-modules/) (only available to Continia partners).

To have one or more modules added to your Document Capture license, please contact [your dedicated Continia partner](@DC-15).

> [!NOTE]
> The base module for Document Capture is mandatory, and you'll be charged according to the number of modules added by your partner.
