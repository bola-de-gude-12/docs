# Modules for Continia Document Capture

Continia Document Capture comes with a number of modules (sometimes referred to as *granules*) that each represent an area of functionality which you may or may not need, depending on your geographical location and your organizational needs and wants. For a list of these and all additional Continia modules, see [ISV modules to add to NAV/Business Central license](https://partnerzone.continia.com/isv-modules-to-add-to-navbusiness-central-license/) (only available to Continia partners).

To have one or more modules added to your Document Capture license, please contact [your dedicated Continia partner](@DC-15).

> [!NOTE]
> The base module for Document Capture is mandatory, and you'll be charged according to the number of modules added by your partner.
