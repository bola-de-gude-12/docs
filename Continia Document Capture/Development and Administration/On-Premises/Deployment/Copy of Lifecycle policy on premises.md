# Software lifecycle policy and Continia Document Capture on premises

Continia provides ongoing servicing and support of all Document Capture on-premises releases for at least two years after each release date. This includes security updates, bug fixes, new features, partner support, and access to online resources including Continia Docs. However, note that all online resources are based on the latest version of Document Capture – meaning that older functionality that has been changed, replaced, or deleted in the meantime is unlikely to be covered. For more information on the various types of support provided by Continia, see \[Resources for Help and Support]\(/Continia Document Capture/Getting Started/Help and Support.md).

All Continia code, including bug fixes, is released for RTM versions of Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, not for cumulative Microsoft updates. If you're interested in implementing a specific bug fix, you must install the service pack that it's part of, as individual patches and bug fixes can't be provided separately. Service packs are released frequently to keep your software up to date and fully functional, and it's strongly recommended that you stay current by installing the latest service packs. For information on how to do so, see [Implementing Service Packs](../../../../Continia%20Document%20Capture/Development%20and%20Administration/On-Premises/Deployment/@DC-167/).

## Releases

Continia releases new major versions of Document Capture and other solutions twice a year: on April 1 and October 1. Between one and two weeks before the official release of every new major version, a preview version is made available in the Business Central online sandbox environments.

> \[!NOTE] With its 2024 spring release, Document Capture made a version leap from 12.00 to 24.00, meaning that versions 13.00–23.00 don't exist and never will. This was done to align the versioning of Document Capture with that of Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central.

Below is an overview of support start and end dates for each major release:

\


| Version                          | Release date | Support end date |
| -------------------------------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Document Capture 2025 R1 (26.00) | Apr 1, 2025  | Oct 1, 2027      |
| Document Capture 2024 R2 (25.00) | Oct 1, 2024  | Oct 1, 2026      |
| Document Capture 2024 R1 (24.00) | Apr 2, 2024  | Apr 2, 2026      |
| Document Capture 2023 R2 (12.00) | Oct 1, 2023  | Oct 1, 2025      |
