# Document categories

Continia Document Capture comes with a number of default document categories, which enable you to classify and organize incoming documents based on their type and purpose.

## Functional areas

Each category is configured to handle a specific document type, from contact-related documents to sales orders and credit memos.

* [The Contact-related Documents category](@DC-433) - manage documents related to your business contacts.
* [The Customers, Employees, and Vendors categories](@DC-239) - automatically attach documents to a person or company in Business Central.
* [The G/L Documents category](@DC-352) - OCR-process and add documents to G/L entries as attachments.
* [The Posted Purchase Receipts category](@DC-174) - OCR-process receipts related to the purchase of goods or services, such as delivery notes or bills of lading.
* [The Purchase Order category](@DC-136) - automatically create and update purchase orders based on data recognized in another document.
* [The Sales Orders and Credit Memos category](@DC-459) - automatically create and update sales orders and credit memos based on data recognized in another document.
