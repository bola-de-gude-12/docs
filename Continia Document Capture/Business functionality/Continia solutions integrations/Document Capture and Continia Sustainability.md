# Document Capture and Business Central Sustainability

{{include "cs-integration" "Document Capture"}}

## Additional functionality

The integration between Document Capture and Business Central Sustainability provides you with sustainability fields, which can be configured on the G/L account, item card, or template levels. These fields can then be populated with values related to your captured documents, and the data within these fields can be imported into purchase invoice lines to help you track your company's emissions.

For information on how to add the environmental fields to Document Capture, see [Tracking emissions in Document Capture](@DC-232).
