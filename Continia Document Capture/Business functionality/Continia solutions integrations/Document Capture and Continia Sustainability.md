# Document Capture and Continia Sustainability

{{include "cs-integration" "Document Capture"}}

## Additional functionality

The integration between Document Capture and Continia Sustainability provides you with environmental fields, which can be configured on the G/L account or template levels. These fields can then be populated with values related to your captured documents, and the data within these fields can be imported into Continia Sustainability to help you track your company's emissions.

For information on how to add the environmental fields to Document Capture, see [Tracking emissions in Document Capture](@DC-232).
