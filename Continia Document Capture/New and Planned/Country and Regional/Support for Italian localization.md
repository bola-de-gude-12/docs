# Support for Italian Localization

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Support for Italian localization | - | - |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> At the moment, this feature isn't planned for development. However, it may be revisited in the future if Continia deems it relevant to do so.

## Business value
Having Continia Document Capture localized into Italian makes it accessible to Italian-speaking users.

## Feature details
With this change, Document Capture will be available in Italy, in the Italian language, and with local Italian Microsoft changes.
