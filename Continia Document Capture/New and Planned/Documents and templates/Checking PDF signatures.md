# Checking PDF Signatures

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Checking PDF signatures | - | N/A |

>[!NOTE]
>As Continia has yet to see legal or business demands regarding the checking of signatures on incoming PDFs, this feature has been postponed to a later version of Document Capture.

## Business value

By enabling Continia Document Capture to check the signatures of incoming PDF documents like invoices and credit memos, this feature will help to prevent fraud. It may also help you meet legal requirements in France, where companies are obliged by law to sign PDF invoices and credit memos. As such requirements are likely to also be introduced in other countries in the near future, the feature will be implemented in a way that makes it universally applicable – not specific to France.

## Feature details

A new vendor template setup option will be introduced, enabling you to set up Document Capture to check if incoming PDF documents have been signed correctly. The setup will also include an option that allows you to make it mandatory for PDFs to be signed.
