# Overview of detailed changelogs

Each time Continia Document Capture is updated, a detailed changelog is published. This changelog lists new or changed functionality and bug fixes included in the release. Each item includes a brief release note explaining the purpose and impact of the change.

Changelogs are also available for the Continia Web Approval Portal, Continia Delivery Network, Document Capture Service, and Continia Docs.

* [Continia Document Capture 2025 R1](@DC-447)
* [Continia Document Capture 2024 R2](@DC-234)
* [Continia Document Capture 2024 R1](@DC-188)

To see a previous changelog, use the table of contents on the left-hand side of the page to navigate directly to the relevant section. If you're on a mobile device, tap the **Content** button in the top-left corner of the page.

> [!TIP]
> As a Continia partner, you can be notified of new Document Capture versions and service packs when they're released. To sign up for this service, see [Receive a notification upon new Document Capture releases](https://continia.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360005355079-Receive-a-notification-upon-new-Document-Capture-releases) (only available to partners).

> [!NOTE]
> With its 2024 spring release, Document Capture made a version leap from 12.00 to 24.00, meaning that versions 13.00–23.00 don't exist and never will. This was done to align the versioning of Document Capture with that of Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central.
