# Ability to Add Missing Order Lines for Matching Purposes

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Ability to add missing order lines for matching purposes | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2020 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2020 | - |

## Feature details
It's now possible to easily add lines to purchase orders during matching, using the new **Create Line on Purchase Document** feature. This is useful if an invoice contains one or more lines that are missing from the corresponding purchase order. Whenever this is the case, the invoice and the order won't match, but matching the two documents will identify the missing line(s) using line recognition, thereby making it easy for you to inject the line(s) into the purchase order.

Such additional purchase order lines may, for example, be created for freight charges, handling fees, or other types of extra charges that may appear in invoices.
