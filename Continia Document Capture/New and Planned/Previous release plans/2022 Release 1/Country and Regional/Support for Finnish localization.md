# Support for Finnish Localization

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Support for Finnish localization |{{checkmark}} Mar 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |

## Business value
Having Continia Document Capture localized into Finnish makes it accessible to Finnish-speaking users.

## Feature details
With this change, Document Capture will be available in Finland, in the Finnish language, and with local Finnish Microsoft changes.
