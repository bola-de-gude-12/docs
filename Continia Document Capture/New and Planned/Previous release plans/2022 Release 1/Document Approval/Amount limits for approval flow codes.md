# Amount Limits for Approval Flow Codes

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Amount limits for approval flow codes |{{checkmark}} Mar 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |

## Business value
By ensuring that only the necessary number of people are involved in the approval process, this feature will enable you to approve documents faster.

## Feature details
The feature will make it possible for you to configure approval flow codes to release documents whenever a certain approval level is met. In this way, each document for approval won't have to be forwarded to all approvers in the flow if it can be released by an approver with the appropriate approval limit early in the process.
