# Attaching Files from the Approval Entries Page

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Attaching files from the Approval Entries page |{{checkmark}} Mar 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |

## Business value
This feature will make it significantly easier to attach additional files during the approval process.

## Feature details
It will be possible to attach documents and other files to invoices and credit notes directly from the **Approval Entries** page, thereby making it unnecessary to open each individual record in order to do so. This makes for faster approvals and an overall smoother user experience.
