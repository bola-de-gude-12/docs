# Drag-and-Drop Functionality for Additional Business Central Pages

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Drag-and-drop functionality for additional Business Central pages |{{checkmark}} Mar 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |

## Supported versions
The feature will be available for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central 2019 release wave 2 (BC v15) and later.

## Business value
The Continia Document Capture drag-and-drop functionality will be expanded to include a number of additional standard Business Central pages, making it easier for you to attach files to posted documents in Business Central.

## Feature details
The drag-and-drop FactBox will be added to the following pages:

* **Posted Sales Invoices** (page 132)
* **Posted Sales Shipments** (page 130)
* **Posted Sales Credit Memos** (page 134)
* **Posted Purchase Receipts** (page 136)
