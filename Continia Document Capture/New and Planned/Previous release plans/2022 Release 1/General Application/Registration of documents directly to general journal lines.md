#Registration of Documents Directly to General Journal Lines

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Registration of documents directly to general journal lines | {{checkmark}} Mar 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |

##Business value
This feature will be particularly useful for businesses that don't require document approvals or detailed posting information for certain purchase documents. Some documents, often from one-time vendors, are unnecessary to register in full in Continia Document Capture and may not contain enough details to warrant a full registration anyway. Such documents may as well be registered directly as lines in the general journal instead, and this feature enables you to do just that.

##Feature details
The feature makes it possible to register documents – without vendor names and other standard details – directly to general journal lines, thereby creating lines in the general journal instead of invoices or credit memos.
