# Improved Line Recognition

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Improved line recognition | {{checkmark}} Mar 2024 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2024 |

## Business value

Building on top of line data captured by artificial intelligence (AI) – and harnessing the power of this – this feature will ensure that more data is captured in the lines of incoming purchase documents. The accuracy of the captured data will also improve considerably.

## Feature details

The line recognition feature of Continia Document Capture will be significantly improved. The new functionality will build on the results of the AI-driven optical character recognition (OCR) that already exists in Document Capture, and it will allow you to manually capture any captions and values that the AI OCR has failed to identify.

For more information, see [Enhanced Line Recognition](@DC-191).
