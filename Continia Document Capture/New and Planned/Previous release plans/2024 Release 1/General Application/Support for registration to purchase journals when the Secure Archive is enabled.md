# Support for Registration to Purchase Journals when the Secure Archive is Enabled

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: | 
| Support for registration to purchase journals when the Secure Archive is enabled | {{checkmark}} Mar 2024 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2024 |

## Business value

It's currently not possible to register documents as lines in a purchase journal while having the Secure Archive enabled. With the addition of this feature, you'll be able to enjoy the benefits of the Secure Archive while registering your documents to purchase journals. This may be useful if you don't need document approvals and detailed posting information for your purchase documents. 

## Feature details

The Secure Archive functionality that's available for purchase documents (including hash checks and document log entries) will be expanded to also cover the registration of documents to purchase journals.

For more information, see [Secure Archive](@DC-154#the-scope-of-the-archive).
