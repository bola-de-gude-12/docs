# Easier Migration from On-Premises to Online for Older Versions

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: | 
| Easier migration from on-premises to online for older versions | {{checkmark}} Mar 2024 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2024 |

## Business value

Currently, if you want to migrate from an on-premises version of Microsoft Dynamics NAV/365 Business Central to Business Central online, you must first upgrade to the latest available version of Continia Document Capture and possibly also to the latest version of NAV/Business Central, depending on your setup. This won't be necessary with this feature, which means a lot less hassle for you.

## Feature details

The architecture used for upgrading to online versions of Business Central will be modified, so that you no longer have to upgrade Document Capture or NAV/Business Central before migrating from NAV/Business Central on-premises to Business Central online.

For more information, see [Migrating Document Capture from Business Central On-Premises to Cloud](@DC-106).
