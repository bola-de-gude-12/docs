# Ability to Copy Out-of-Office Settings to All Companies

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Ability to copy out-of-office settings to all companies | {{checkmark}} Mar 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Apr 1, 2021 | - |

## Business value
This feature makes it much easier and faster for you to register out-of-office settings for your entire organization in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, as you can do it in one go. It also ensures accuracy, seeing that you only have to enter your out-of-office details once rather than multiple times.

## Feature details
When setting up your out-of-office assistant, you'll be able to copy your out-of-office settings to all companies.
