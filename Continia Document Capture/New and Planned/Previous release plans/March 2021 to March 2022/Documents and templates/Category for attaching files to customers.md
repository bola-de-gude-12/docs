# Category for Attaching Files to Customers

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Category for attaching files to customers | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2021 | - |

## Business value
Having all relevant documents easily available in one place is generally desirable, as you'll save valuable time looking for them. 

## Feature details
For easier registration, categorization, and lookup of documents, this feature will enable you to automatically attach OCR-processed PDF files directly to your customers in Continia Document Capture.
