## Business value
With the Continia Delivery Network, you can integrate with and gather all of your data exchange networks in one place. It's easy for you to register your company as a recipient or sender of electronic documents in multiple networks, meaning that you get one cloud solution that simplifies the process of setting up and connecting with these – as opposed to having multiple contracts with different service providers using different types of software that you'd have to install, update, and otherwise maintain. The Continia Delivery Network renders all of this unnecessary by offering one consolidated service, and you won't even have to upgrade or be tech-savvy to use it.

## Feature details
The Continia Delivery Network is a service that integrates with the PEPPOL eDelivery Network, thereby enabling vendors to import or export business documents via this. In the future, other electronic data exchange networks such as NemHandel are likely to be supported as well.

If, for example, some of your vendors are already connected to the PEPPOL eDelivery Network, they can send documents into Continia Document Capture via this network using the Continia Delivery Network.

The Continia Delivery Network will be fully integrated with other Continia products, including Document Capture, so there's no need for you to learn anything new or set up any additional folders. Once your company is registered in the Continia Delivery Network, all incoming electronic documents will appear in exactly the same way as PDF files that are sent to you via email – no need for extra setup or any other software.
