# Warnings About Invoices Already Paid and Registered in Continia Expense Management

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Warnings about invoices already paid and registered in Continia Expense Management | {{checkmark}} Sep 2024 | {{checkmark}} Oct 2024 |

## Business value

By identifying any duplicate transactions in Continia Expense Management, this feature will prevent you from accidentally posting – or perhaps even paying – the same invoices twice.

## Feature details

For each incoming invoice, Continia Document Capture will attempt to determine if a corresponding transaction already exists in Expense Management. If so, a warning will be displayed in the **Comments** section of the document journal. You'll be able to configure the comment type for added flexibility.
