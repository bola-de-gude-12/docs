# New line type – Allocation Accounts

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| New line type – **Allocation Accounts** | {{checkmark}} Sep 2024 | {{checkmark}} Oct 2024 |

## Business value

With the implementation of this feature, Continia Document Capture will support even more standard Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central functionality.

## Feature details

The standard Business Central [allocation account](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dynamics365/business-central/finance-allocate-revenue-costs) feature will now be supported by Document Capture. This means that you'll be able to select **Allocation Account** in the following fields during line recognition:

* On the **Document Card**: In **Translate to Type** (and **Translate to No.**)
* On the **Accounts for Amounts** page: In **Type**

Allocation accounts will be supported in the Continia Web Approval Portal at a later date.
