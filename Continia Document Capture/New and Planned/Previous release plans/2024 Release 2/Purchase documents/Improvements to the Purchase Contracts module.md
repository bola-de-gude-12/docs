# Improvements to the Purchase Contracts Module

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Improvements to the Purchase Contracts module | {{checkmark}} Sep 2024 | {{checkmark}} Oct 2024 |

## Business value

This feature will ensure a higher level of automation for the purchase contract review process, which will be triggered more frequently if needed. It will also improve Continia Document Capture's ability to determine if incoming invoices are recurring and suitable for automatic handling by a purchase contract.

## Feature details

The feature will bring the following two overall improvements to the Purchase Contracts module:

1. Improved review functionality, similar to the existing document approval functionality. This includes various rules, restrictions, and conditions for when to trigger the review process.
1. Improvements to the linking of documents and purchase contracts.
