# Support for purchase advance letters in the Czech Republic

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Support for purchase advance letters in the Czech Republic | {{checkmark}} Sep 2024 | {{checkmark}} Oct 2024 |

## Business value

A purchase advance letter is a type of document that's used in the Czech Republic. With the introduction of this feature, purchase advance letters will be supported by Continia Document Capture.

## Feature details

In the Czech Republic, the feature will ensure that documents can be registered as purchase advance letters using Document Capture.
