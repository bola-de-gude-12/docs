# Added Support for the Transfer of Invoice Discount Amounts

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Added support for the transfer of invoice discount amounts | {{checkmark}} Sep 2024 | {{checkmark}} Oct 2024 |

## Business value

With the implementation of this feature, Continia Document Capture will support even more standard Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central functionality.

## Feature details

A new template field, **Invoice Discount Amount**, will be added to the master template in the **PURCHASE** category. If this field is populated during capture, the value will be transferred to the standard **Invoice Discount Amount** field in Business Central when the document is registered.
