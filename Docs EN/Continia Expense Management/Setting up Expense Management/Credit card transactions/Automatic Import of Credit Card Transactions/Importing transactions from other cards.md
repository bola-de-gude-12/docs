# Importing Transactions from Other Cards 
Continia supports the automatic import of transactions from numerous different types of credit cards directly into Continia Expense Management, including major credit cards like Mastercard, Visa, American Express, and Eurocard. If you're unsure whether Continia supports the credit card(s) used in your organization, please contact [your Continia partner](/Continia Expense Management/Getting Started/Resellers and Partners/Find a Reseller.md). Your partner will be able to tell you if Continia already offers support for your credit card(s), or if additional steps must be taken.
