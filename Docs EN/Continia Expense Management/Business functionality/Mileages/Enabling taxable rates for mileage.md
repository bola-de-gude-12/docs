# Enabling Taxable Rates for Mileage

In this article, you'll learn how to enable taxable rates for mileage and specify taxable amounts for mileage rates in case your company wants to reimburse its employees more than the tax-free rates set by the government.

## Enabling taxable rates for mileage

To enable taxable rates for mileage, follow these steps:

1. Choose the {{search}} icon, enter **Expense Management Setup**, and then choose the related link.
1. In the **Mileage** FastTab, select **Enable Taxable Rates**.

### Specifying taxable amounts for mileage rates

For taxable rates to work, you also need to specify taxable amounts for mileage rates, and here's how you do this:

1. Choose the {{search}} icon, enter **Mileage Rates**, and then choose the related link.
1. On the action bar, select **Edit List**.
1. In the **Taxable Rate (LCY)** column, specify the taxable rate.
