# Introducing Cash Advance

| Feature                                  | General availability on-premises | General availability online |     Public preview     |
| ---------------------------------------- | :------------------------------: | :-------------------------: | :--------------------: |
| Cash Advance expense type & payment type |      {{checkmark}} Apr 2025      |   {{checkmark}} Apr 2025    | {{checkmark}} Mar 2025 |

## Business value
The Cash Advance expense type enhances user experience by bringing clarity and efficiency to expense reporting. This new feature simplifies the cash advance and cash withdrawal process, so there is no need for additional configuration. 

## Feature details
 - The Cash Advance expense type is now available out-of-the-box, ensuring a clear and straightforward option for users. This includes reporting cash withdrawals.

- Users can quickly select Cash Advance when reporting expenses, making the process more intuitive and efficient. It also allows for easy reporting of cash withdrawals.
- Administrators save time as there is no need to manually set up this expense type.
- The Cash Advance expense type provides a standardized method for managing cash advances and withdrawals, reducing the risk of errors, and ensuring compliance with company policies.

For more information see, [Using Cash Advance](@EM-304).
