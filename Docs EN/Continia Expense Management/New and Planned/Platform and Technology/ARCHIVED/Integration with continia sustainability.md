# Integration with Continia Sustainability

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |

## Business value

Continia Sustainability is the latest addition to the Continia product suite, specifically designed to manage your company's carbon accounting. When integrated with Continia Expense Management, this combination becomes an even more powerful tool for your organization. 

Expense Management streamlines the process by allowing employees to submit all necessary documentation related to their expenses, which directly contributes to accurate carbon accounting and CO2 footprint measurement.

This integration creates a robust solution for tomorrow's business needs, enabling companies to seamlessly track and manage both financial expenses and environmental impact, driving sustainability and operational efficiency.

## Feature details

This feature enables seamless correlation, transformation, and posting of Expense Management data into carbon accounts. Expense Management provides a user-friendly platform and mobile app interface for employees to effortlessly register their expenses. 

Additionally, it offers the flexibility to add custom field types that are linked to various carbon accounts, all of which can be selected directly within the mobile app.

Building on its current capabilities, the solution ensures full automation – simply choose an expense type, configure the posting setup, and let the system handle the rest.
