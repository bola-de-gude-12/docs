# Document Storage in Continia Online

## Feature details
Expense documents can now be stored in the cloud using Continia Online. Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central is still the central point of storage, but when expense documents are stored in Continia Online, expense users can always view their submitted expense documents in the Expense Portal, no matter what device or browser they use or where in the world they're located. Even if they get a new mobile device, all submitted expense documents are still available and viewable.
