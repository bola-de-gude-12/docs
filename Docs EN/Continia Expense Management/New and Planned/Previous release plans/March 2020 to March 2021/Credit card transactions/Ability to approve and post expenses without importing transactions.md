# Ability to Approve and Post Expenses Without Importing Transactions

## Feature details
Continia Expense Management 4.00.01 made it possible to approve and post expenses in cases where expense users have used a corporate credit card and it's impossible to import transactions from the bank or credit card company into Continia Expense Management. In earlier versions, such expenses must be matched with a transaction before being approved and posted.
