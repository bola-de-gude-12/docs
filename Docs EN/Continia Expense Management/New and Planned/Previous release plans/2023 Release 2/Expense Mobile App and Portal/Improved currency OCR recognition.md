# Improved currency OCR recognition by matching against ISO codes

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |

## Business value
This Expense Management enhancement improves currency OCR recognition. By mapping currency codes to ISO codes, users will unlock the full potential of OCR capabilities, leading to more accurate and efficient expense processing.

## Feature details
With this enhancement, Expense Management will gain enhanced recognition capabilities for currency codes when using OCR.
