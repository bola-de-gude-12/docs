# Notifications in the Expense App

## Business value
With notifications directly in the Expense App, expense users are now notified immediately when a document changes status.

## Feature details
The feature will enable push notifications in the Expense App, meaning that notifications will be sent straight to the relevant expense user whenever important document changes require the user's attention.
