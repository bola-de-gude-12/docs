As environmental challenges intensify, the concept of business success is evolving to prioritize sustainability. Climate change, caused by excess greenhouse gases (GHG), is disrupting our climate and pushing us to find a balance between emissions and the Earth’s natural ability to absorb them. 

By integrating Business Central Sustainability with Continia {param1}, you can more effectively track and manage your climate impact – enabling your business to comply with regulations while contributing to a more sustainable future.

To learn more about Business Central Sustainability, see [Sustainability management overview](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dynamics365/business-central/finance-manage-sustainability) (Microsoft article).
