> [!IMPORTANT]
>
> **End-of-sale date for Payment Management**
>
> Starting **1 October 2025**, Continia will discontinue new sales of Payment Management. Additionally, as of **1 October 2026**, the solution will no longer be supported, and no further updates or new versions will be released.
>
> To ensure a smooth transition, we kindly ask clients currently using Payment Management to migrate to [Continia Banking](https://docs.continia.com/en-us/continia-banking/) by **1 October 2026**. This is especially important for Business Central online users, as we cannot guarantee that Payment Management will continue to work in that environment after this date. 
>
> We also strongly encourage on-premises users to upgrade to Continia Banking, as support for Payment Management will be fully phased out. Migrating to Continia Banking ensures uninterrupted access to essential banking features going forward.
>
> If you have any questions or need assistance with the migration, please don’t hesitate to [reach out](https://docs.continia.com/en-us/continia-banking/getting-started/help-and-support/) — we are here to help.
>
> Note for clients in Great Britain: please contact your Partner Account Manager for guidance on this transition.
