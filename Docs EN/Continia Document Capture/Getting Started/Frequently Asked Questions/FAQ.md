# General FAQ
In this section, you can find answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about Continia Document Capture. The answers provide information on anything from availability to zero-cost trials, and also include multiple links to other sites with more information.

## Is Document Capture available in my country?
Document Capture is available in a range of countries and languages. To access the full lists, use the links below:

* [Country/Regional Availability and Supported Languages](@DC-1) (app)
* [Country/Regional Availability and Supported Languages for FOB](@DC-2) (FOB)

## How do I get Document Capture?
To ensure that you get the best possible service, Continia operates through a network of highly specialized Continia partners. These partners, who are all official Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central partners, know everything there is to know about Document Capture and Business Central in general – and can get you up and running in no time. Reach out to your dedicated Continia partner to get started right away.

See also: [Finding a reseller of Continia Document Capture](/Continia Document Capture/Getting Started/Resellers and Partners/Find a Reseller.md).

You can also start with a free 30-day trial or even set up a sandbox environment. For more information, see [Continia Document Capture Trials and Subscriptions](/Continia Document Capture/Getting Started/Trials and Subscriptions.md).

## Where do I go if I have questions?
If you have any questions about Document Capture that you can't find answers to in this documentation, you’re more than welcome to ask your Continia partner.

For more information, see [Resources for Help and Support](/Continia Document Capture/Getting Started/Help and Support.md) and [Resellers and Partners](/Continia Document Capture/Getting Started/Resellers and Partners/Overview.md).

## What does it cost to use Document Capture?
The cost of using Document Capture for Business Central online depends on how many modules you choose and how many documents you register per month. For Business Central on-premises, the cost is based on other parameters, which you can find in the link below.

You can find detailed information on pricing for both online and on-premises usage on [Continia Pricing](https://www.continia.com/pricing/).

## How's the connection between Business Central and Continia Online secured?

The connection between Business Central and Continia Online is protected by [Transport Layer Security (TLS) 1.2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transport_Layer_Security).
