# Managing electronic documents in Document Capture
Exchanging electronic documents directly with Continia Document Capture is the straightforward way to optimize your business. More and more public authorities all over the world are mandating electronic exchange of documents, and this is also becoming increasingly popular among business-to-business organizations, which is why it's an essential part of Document Capture.

Your options for exchanging electronic documents with Document Capture depend on which types of electronic documents and files you're exchanging. You can easily exchange files by connecting to the Continia Delivery Network. Additionally, you can send files to an email address monitored by Document Capture.

The Continia Delivery Network is a service that integrates with the Peppol eDelivery Network, thereby enabling you to send and receive electronic documents directly in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. Continia is a certified Peppol access point, which eliminates the need for any third-party vendors to integrate your company into the Peppol eDelivery Network. When you sign up to the Continia Delivery Network, you're automatically set up as a recipient in the global Peppol eDelivery Network. To learn more about how to connect to the Peppol eDelivery Network or the NemHandel network, see [Setting up the Continia Delivery Network](@DC-75).  

When using an email address monitored by Document Capture, you can import electronic documents in many formats. Most widespread formats are supported, but not all. For a list of supported formats, see [Supported formats](@DC-55). 

To learn more about how Continia supports different countries as an access point and the current regulatory state and future outlook, select your country in the table of contents.
