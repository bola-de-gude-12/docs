# Overview of importing electronic documents
Importing electronic documents directly into Continia Document Capture is the straightforward way to optimize your business. More and more public authorities all over the world are mandating electronic exchange of documents, and this is also becoming increasingly popular among business-to-business organizations, which is why it's an essential part of Document Capture.

Your options for importing electronic documents into Document Capture depend on which types of electronic documents and files you're importing. You can import files either by sending them to an email address monitored by Document Capture or by connecting to the Continia Delivery Network.

The Continia Delivery Network is a service that integrates with the Peppol eDelivery Network, thereby enabling you to receive electronic purchase invoices and credit memos directly in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. Continia is a certified Peppol access point, which eliminates the need for any third-party vendors to integrate your company into the Peppol eDelivery Network. When you sign up to the Continia Delivery Network, you're automatically set up as a recipient in the global Peppol eDelivery Network.
