# Support for the PINT Billing and PINT A-NZ Billing formats in APAC

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Support for the PINT Billing and PINT A-NZ Billing formats in APAC | {{checkmark}} May 2025 | {{checkmark}} May 2025 |

>[!NOTE]
>This feature was released in May 2025, in line with the requirements by the Australian and New Zealand governments.

## Business value

This feature will allow you to meet the APAC requirement of sending and receiving business documents in PINT formats using Continia eDocuments, including extensions like the A-NZ format. You can read more about the legal requirements in [Managing electronic documents in Australia](@DC-202) and [Managing electronic documents in New Zealand](@DC-201).

## Feature details

With the implementation of the feature, Continia eDocuments will support the sending and receipt of eDocuments in the PINT Billing and PINT A-NZ Billing formats using the Continia Delivery Network. This will only apply to invoices and credit memos.

For more information, see [Understanding PINT and PINT A-NZ for eInvoicing in Australia and New Zealand](@DC-463).
