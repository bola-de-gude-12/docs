# Purchase Contracts Trial

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Purchase Contracts trial | - | {{checkmark}} Oct 2023 |

## Business value

Having the option to try out Purchase Contracts for a whole month at no additional cost and without committing to anything at all will allow you to fully explore the benefits of the module. If you're somewhat unsure about whether to acquire the module on a permanent basis, this trial option will enable you to make a truly informed decision.

## Feature details

The Purchase Contracts trial option will enable you to try out the Purchase Contracts module in your production environment free of charge for 30 days. You'll be able to easily activate your free 30-day trial either from the **Continia Solution Management** page or via in-app notifications, in case the system detects document patterns which indicate that you could benefit from using the Purchase Contracts module.

For more information, see [Overview of Purchase Contracts](@DC-85).
