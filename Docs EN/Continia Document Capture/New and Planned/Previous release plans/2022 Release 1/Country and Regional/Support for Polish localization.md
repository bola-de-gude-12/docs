# Support for Polish Localization

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Support for Polish localization |{{checkmark}} Mar 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |

## Business value
Having Continia Document Capture localized into Polish makes it accessible to Polish-speaking users.

## Feature details
With this change, Document Capture will be available in Poland, in the Polish language, and with local Polish Microsoft changes.
