# Support for Portuguese Localization

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Support for Portuguese localization | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |

## Business value
Having Continia Document Capture localized into Portuguese makes it accessible to Portuguese-speaking users.

## Feature details
With this change, Document Capture will be available in Portugal, in the Portuguese language, and with local Portuguese Microsoft changes.
