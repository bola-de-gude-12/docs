# Highlighting only relevant lines for advanced approval

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Highlighting only relevant lines for advanced approval | {{checkmark}} Sep 2024 | {{checkmark}} Oct 2024 |

## Business value

By highlighting only relevant lines for approval in the Continia Web Approval Portal, this feature will enable you to filter out any unnecessary noise and focus on exactly what concerns you. This will significantly streamline your workflow.

## Feature details

In the Web Approval Portal, the lines that are relevant for you to approve will be highlighted to make them easier for you to spot. This will only apply to advanced approval.
