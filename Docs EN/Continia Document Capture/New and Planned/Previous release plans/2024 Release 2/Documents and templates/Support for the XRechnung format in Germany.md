# Support for the XRechnung Format in Germany

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Support for the XRechnung format in Germany | {{checkmark}} Sep 2024 | {{checkmark}} Oct 2024 |

## Business value

This feature will allow you to meet the German requirement of sending and receiving business documents in the XRechnung format using Continia eDocuments. You can read more about the legal requirements in [Managing Electronic Documents in Germany](@DC-195).

## Feature details

With the implementation of the feature, Continia eDocuments will support the sending and receipt of eDocuments in the German XRechnung format using the Continia Delivery Network. This will only apply to invoices and credit memos.
