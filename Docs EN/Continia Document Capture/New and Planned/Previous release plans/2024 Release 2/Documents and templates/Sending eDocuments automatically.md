# Sending eDocuments Automatically

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Sending eDocuments automatically | {{checkmark}} Sep 2024 | {{checkmark}} Oct 2024 |

## Business value

With the introduction of this feature, the sending of business documents as eDocuments will be automated. This means that you avoid the hassle of having to manually select **Send** for each individual document, resulting in a smoother and more streamlined workflow.

## Feature details

The feature will allow documents to be sent automatically when certain actions are performed. For example, you'll be able to have sales invoices sent when they're posted or purchase orders when they're released.
