# Support for Assignment of Item Charges During Document Registration

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Support for assignment of item charges during document registration | {{checkmark}} Mar 2023 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2023 |

## Business value

Having item charges assigned automatically by Continia Document Capture will save you considerable amounts of time and effort. All you'll have to do is specify the assignment method in the document template, and Document Capture will then take care of the actual assignment for you.

## Feature details

The feature will enable Document Capture to assign item charges to the lines of an incoming document in accordance with the method you've defined in the relevant template (according to quantity, weight, etc.). The charges will be automatically assigned as part of the registration of the document.
