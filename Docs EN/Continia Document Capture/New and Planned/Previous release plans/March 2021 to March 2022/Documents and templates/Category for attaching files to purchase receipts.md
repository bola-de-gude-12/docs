# Category for Attaching Files to Purchase Receipts

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Category for attaching files to purchase receipts | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2021 | - |

## Business value
This feature is particularly useful to businesses that require vendor delivery notes or bills of lading to be easily accessible directly from their purchase receipt cards. Having such relevant documents linked to your posted purchase receipts makes it very easy for you to retrieve them.

## Feature details
With this new document category, you can OCR-process documents and attach them to posted purchase receipts in the same flow.
