#Addition of the Drag-and-Drop Feature to the Document Card and the Document Journal

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Addition of the drag-and-drop feature to the document card and the document journal | {{checkmark}} Mar 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Apr 1, 2021 | - |

##Business value
This feature will drastically reduce the time you spend adding additional documentation to your Continia Document Capture documents. It enables you to attach any relevant file to any document simply by dragging and dropping the file directly onto the document in question.

##Feature details
The drag-and-drop FactBox will be added to document cards and the document journal, enabling you to easily attach files to documents using drag and drop. The actual attachments will be moved to the created purchase documents during registration.
