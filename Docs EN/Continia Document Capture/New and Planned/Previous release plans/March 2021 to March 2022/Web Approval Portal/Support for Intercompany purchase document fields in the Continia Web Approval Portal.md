# Support for Intercompany Purchase Document Fields in the Continia Web Approval Portal

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Support for Intercompany purchase document fields in the Continia Web Approval Portal | {{checkmark}} Mar 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Apr 1, 2021 | - |

## Business value
With this feature, approvers will be able to take full advantage of the powerful Intercompany functionality in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, without having to sign in to Business Central.

## Feature details
Support will be added for Intercompany purchase document fields in the Continia Web Approval Portal.

More specifically, the Intercompany account fields **IC Partner No.**, **IC Partner Ref. Type**, and **IC Partner Ref. No.** will be made available on document lines in the Web Approval Portal. This will enable approvers to fully specify Intercompany-related information in purchase documents during the approval process.
