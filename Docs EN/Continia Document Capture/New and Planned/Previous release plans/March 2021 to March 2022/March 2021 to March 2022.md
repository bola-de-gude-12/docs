# Features Planned and Released Between March 2021 and March 2022

This article lists the major Continia Document Capture features that were released between March 2021 and March 2022.

| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |

| [Support for Intercompany purchase document fields in the Continia Web Approval Portal](@DC-382) | - | {{checkmark}} Mar 2021 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2021 |
| [Ability to copy out-of-office settings to all companies](@DC-383) | - | {{checkmark}} Mar 2021 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2021 |
