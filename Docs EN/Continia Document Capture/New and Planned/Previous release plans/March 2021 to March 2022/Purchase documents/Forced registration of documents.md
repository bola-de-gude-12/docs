# Forced Registration of Documents

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Forced registration of documents | {{checkmark}} Mar 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Apr 1, 2021 | - |

## Business value
This feature will significantly reduce document handling time, for example for one-time vendors.

## Feature details
The feature allows authorized users to force the registration of Continia Document Capture documents, enabling them to register invoices or credit notes with a minimum of recognized information (vendor and amounts).
