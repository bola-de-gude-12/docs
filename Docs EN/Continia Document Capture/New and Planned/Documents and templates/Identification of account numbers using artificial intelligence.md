# Identification of Account Numbers Using Artificial Intelligence

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Identification of account numbers using artificial intelligence | - | N/A |

>[!NOTE]
>The development of this feature is on hold. For the moment, it's not worth pursuing it because the identification of account numbers wouldn't benefit from our use of artificial intelligence.

## Business value

By automatically suggesting account numbers for incoming purchase documents, this feature will significantly reduce the processing time of each document. The accuracy of the account numbers is also likely to increase.

## Feature details

Artificial intelligence will be used to identify the correct account number for any incoming purchase document, based on the data contained in the document.
