# Support for Business Central Norway and Finland

| Feature | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Support for Business Central Norway and Finland |      {{checkmark}} Apr 2025 | {{checkmark}} Mar 2025 |

## Business Value

Availability of Continia Banking in Norway and Finland.


## Feature Details

Continia Banking includes key features tailored for the Norwegian localization, such as KID references, and the user interface (UI) is now fully translated into Norwegian. 

Similarly, Continia Banking now supports Finnish localization, including key features designed for the Finnish market and a fully translated user interface.
