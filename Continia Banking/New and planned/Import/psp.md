## Business value

With an extension of the importing services, we enable finance departments to follow the company cash flow with real-time updated customer payment transactions from third-party payment service providers (PSPs).

## Feature details

Continia Banking will allow direct file imports from PSPs, so customer payment files can be easily added to the cash receipt journal. This new feature automatically updates the journals in Business Central with these imported files. Designed to be flexible and user-friendly, it can handle payment files from multiple PSPs, making it adaptable to various formats and scenarios, not limited to specific providers.

For more information, refer to the [Introducing Payment Service Providers](@CB-128) article.
