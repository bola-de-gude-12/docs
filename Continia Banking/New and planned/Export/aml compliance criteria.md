# AML compliance criteria

| Feature | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| AML compliance criteria |        {{checkmark}} Apr 2025        | {{checkmark}} Mar 2025 |

## Business Value

Ensures compliance with anti-money laundering regulations in Norway.


## Feature Details

Supports the AML act by allowing identity information to be attached to each payment sent to the bank. This enables tracking of transactions back to the individual within a company who initiated the payment, ensuring full traceability.

For more information, refer to the [Ensuring AML compliance in Continia Banking](@CB-211) article.
