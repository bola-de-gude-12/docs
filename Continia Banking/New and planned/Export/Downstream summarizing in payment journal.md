# Summarizing payments after creating a payment suggestion

| Feature | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Summarizing payments after creating a payment suggestion |   {{checkmark}} June 2025   | - |

## Business Value

Summarizing transactions in the payment journal will provide a clearer overview of payment activities and improve reporting efficiency, making it easier to track and manage financial data.


## Feature Details

This feature will automatically summarize payment data downstream in the payment journal. It will help reduce the number of individual transactions listed in the journal, simplifying financial reporting and making it easier to analyze payment trends over time.
