# Multiple currency payments from one bank account

| Feature | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Multiple currency payments from one bank account |        Oct 2025        | - |

## Business Value

Allowing multiple currency payments from a single bank account will simplify the management of international payments, reduce the need for additional accounts, and cut down on administrative overhead.


## Feature Details

This feature will enable businesses to process payments in different currencies using a single bank account. The system will automatically handle currency conversions, ensuring that the correct amount is debited or credited in the appropriate currency, and will also maintain accurate exchange rate information.
