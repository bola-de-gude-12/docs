# Overview

Continia Banking supports integration between Business Central and a range of payment service providers (PSPs). This allows you to include third-party payments in your financial processes, from importing payment files to matching transactions with ledger entries. 

>[!TIP]
>We are continuously working on adding support for new payment service providers. If your preferred PSP is not listed yet, contact Continia to discuss possibilities.

## Core concepts and capabilities

The following documentation is available for working with specific payment service providers:

- [Adding a payment service provider](@CB-181) - how to register and configure a PSP agreement.
- [Importing PSP payments](@CB-209) - how to bring PSP payment data into Business Central.
- [Matching ledger entries with PSP payments](@CB-182) - how to reconcile imported payments with ledger entries

>  [!IMPORTANT]
>Only users with the CTS-CB ADMIN, CTS-CBCP ADMIN, and CTS-PP ADMIN permission set can create PSP agreements and import PSP files.
