# Buckaroo

Buckaroo is a Dutch payment service provider that offers a wide range of online payment solutions for businesses. It enables secure, multi-channel payments, including credit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallets. With Continia Banking, you can use the PSP integration feature to send payment data between Buckaroo and Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central.

Buckaroo Payout
