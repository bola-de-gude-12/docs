# Managing employee payments

Continia Banking makes managing employee payments easier with its improved payment journal. It offers helpful features like checking payments for errors and connecting directly to your bank, which simplifies sending payments to your employees.

To use these tools for handling and suggesting employee payments, make sure Continia Banking is activated and set up in your payment journal.

> [!IMPORTANT]
>
> Before creating an employee payment suggestion, you must fill in the fields **Balance Account No.** and the **Payment Method Code** on the [Employee card](@CB-111) for the existing employees.

To create employee payment suggestions:

1. Search ({{search}}) and select **Payment Journals**. 
1. Open the relevant journal, and on the action bar, select **Home** > **Suggest Employee Payments**.
1. Define the relevant criteria for suggesting employee payments.
1. Select **OK** to create the suggested employee payment lines. 

To process employee payments:

*  To process employee payments effectively using Continia Banking’s payment journal, you must first determine the payment purpose:
   *  **Posting Expenses** - if your goal is to post employee expenses that have already been recorded in a bank account, use the payment method *Manual*. This method allows you to update the bank ledger without transferring any funds.
   *  **Transferring Funds** - if you need to actually transfer money to your employees, select a payment method that your bank supports, such as BTD. This ensures that the system properly processes the payment and sends it to your employees' bank accounts.
