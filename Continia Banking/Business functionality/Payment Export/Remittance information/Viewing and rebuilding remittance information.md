# Viewing and rebuilding remittance information

Whether you use the standard remittance information template or a custom one, you can view the remittance details in either the Payment Journal or on the Payment Suggestion page. This article describes how to view and edit remittance information. For more information about adding a custom remittance template, refer to the [Creating a Remittance Information Template](@CB-117) article. To learn more about selecting the remittance information template, refer to the [Selecting a Remittance Information Template](@CB-116) article.

To view remittance information in the Payment Journal:
1. Navigate to the Payment Journals page and select the relevant line.
2. Go to **Line** > **Remittance Information** to access the details. Alternatively, you can view the **Remittance Information Entries** in the right panel. On the **Remittance Information Entries** page, you have the option to edit the list. 

Sometimes, you may need to update the remittance information on a specific line, such as when you modify the remittance information template.

To update remittance information in the Payment Journal:
1. On the Payment Journals page, select the line and go to **Line** > **Rebuild Remittance Information**. 
2. You can now choose to select the line only or all lines. Select **Ok** to proceed. 
   ![Rebuild remittance information](/images/CB/rebuild-remittance-information.png)

To view remittance information on the Payment Suggestion Card:

1. On the Payment Suggestion Card, select **Actions** > **Remittance Information** 
2.  On the **Remittance Information Entries** page, you have the option to edit the list and rebuild the remittance entries.
