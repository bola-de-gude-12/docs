# Overview of setting up Continia Banking

At the installation of Continia Banking, the solution includes standard configurations for most payment-related business processes. The most important functionality will be configured in the mandatory Continia Banking [assisted setup guides](@CB-34). These configurations you can later change to suit the needs of your company. 

For a detailed walkthrough of how to set up the basics for Continia Banking, follow the [Quick Guide to Setting Up Continia Banking](@CB-03).
