# License and Granules for Continia Banking

Continia Banking comes with several modules (sometimes referred to as *granules*) that each represents an area of functionality that you may or may not need, depending on your geographical location and your organizational needs and wants. For a list of these and all additional Continia modules, see [ISV modules to add to NAV/Business Central license](https://partnerzone.continia.com/isv-modules-to-add-to-navbusiness-central-license/) (only available to Continia partners).

To have one or more modules added to your Continia Banking license, please contact [your dedicated Continia partner](@CB-10).

> [!NOTE]
>
> The base module for Continia Banking is mandatory, and you'll be charged according to the number of modules your partner adds.
