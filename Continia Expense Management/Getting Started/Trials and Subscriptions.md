# Continia Expense Management trials and subscriptions
You can use Expense Management in a few different ways, some of which you can also combine. You'll find a description for each of the methods in more detail below.

## Sign up for a trial
If you’d like to try out Expense Management before subscribing, you can sign up for a free trial [here](https://appsource.microsoft.com/en-us/product/DynamicsBC/PUBID.continia365%7CAID.8d4eab29-8c7f-4b6c-be9a-b7fdfb9da196%7CPAPPID.8d4eab29-8c7f-4b6c-be9a-b7fdfb9da196) – select **Free trial** in the upper-left corner. This free trial gives you access to all Expense Management functionality for up to 30 days, or until you've reached the maximum number of registered Expense App users. Following the trial period, or at any point during it, you can subscribe to Expense Management. When you do subscribe, you’ll already be up and running and can just continue working with the same data as were during your trial period.

To familiarize yourself with Expense Management, see [Getting started with Continia Expense Management](Overview.md).

For any questions, reach out to your certified Continia partner. You can find more information [here](Resellers and Partners/Find a Reseller.md).

## Start a subscription
To get the full version of Expense Management on a more permanent basis and with absolutely no limitations or strings attached, you can always subscribe and get started right away. In this way you can start benefiting from the app and using it for real sooner than with a trial, and the user-friendly interface makes it easy to get started.

To subscribe to Expense Management, simply reach out to your existing Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central partner. Your partner will help you with all subscription-related matters and get you up and running fast. In fact, it’s important that you’re assisted by your partner when you activate any version of Expense Management, as this ensures that you get off to a good start and that everything is done properly. For more information, see [Finding a Reseller of Continia Expense Management](Resellers and Partners/Find a Reseller.md).

You can ask your partner any questions you might have. If you’re looking for further inspiration or would like to familiarize yourself with Expense Management, there’s plenty of useful information under [Getting started with Continia Expense Management](Overview.md).

## Set up a sandbox
Looking for a safe way to test and try out things in Expense Management? Then a sandbox may be the way to go for you. Sandboxes let you practice and experiment without the risk of losing any valuable data or breaking anything in production, and you can process documents in them completely free of charge.

Your administrator can set up one or more sandbox accounts alongside your production environment, even if you’ve signed up for a trial. No data is transferred from any of the sandbox accounts to your production environment, so playing around with sandbox data is completely risk-free.

To get started with sandboxes, contact your existing Business Central partner. For more information, see [Finding a Reseller of Continia Expense Management](Resellers and Partners/Find a Reseller.md).
