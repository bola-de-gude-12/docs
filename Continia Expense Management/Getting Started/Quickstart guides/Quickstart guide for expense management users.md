# Quickstart guide for Expense Management users

Use this quickstart guide if you submit expenses and supporting documentation to your company. You will be guided through how to use the Expense Management Mobile App or the online [Continia Expense Portal](https://cem.continiaonline.com/Account/Login) (use the [AU Continia Expense Portal if you're in Australia](https://eun-cem.continiaonline.com/Account/Login)). The articles and videos in the playlist of this quickstart guide have been curated and compiled specifically for you. 

Whether you're new to Expense Management or have been working with it for a while, the following series of articles and videos will help you become familiar with the features of the Expense Management app and portal. You can then use them in accordance with your company requirements, and be able to set up a smooth and efficient workflow.

## Part 1: Understand the Expense Mobile App

Before you can use the online Expense Portal, your admin must create an account for you. To use the Continia Expense Mobile App on the go, you must first download the Continia Expense App on your device from an app store.
