# Country/Regional Availability and Supported Languages for FOB

This page provides an overview of what countries/regions Continia Expense Management is available in and what languages are supported for _FOB_ versions of Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. You can find information on country and language availability for _app_ versions of Business Central [here](../../../Continia%20Expense%20Management/Development%20and%20Administration/On-Premises/@EM-1/).

> \[!NOTE] The latest version of Expense Management to include support for FOB versions of Business Central is Expense Management 2024 R2. This means the following information on countries, regions, and languages will not be updated any further.

## Countries/regions and languages

The following table lists all countries/regions where Expense Management is available, along with the corresponding language/region codes and language tags. Any applicable comments are provided in footnotes.
