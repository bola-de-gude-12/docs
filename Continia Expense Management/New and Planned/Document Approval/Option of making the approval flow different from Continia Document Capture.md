#Option of Making the Approval Flow Different from Continia Document Capture

##Feature details
With the release of Continia Expense Management 6.50, the *expense approver* user role has been added to the **Continia User Setup** page. This makes it possible to create approval flows that are different from the standard Document Capture and Expense Management approval flows. In earlier versions, only Document Capture approvers could be used for Continia Expense Management.
