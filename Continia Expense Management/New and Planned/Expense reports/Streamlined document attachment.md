# Streamlined Document Attachment for Expense Reports in the Expense Portal

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Streamlined document attachment for expense reports | {{checkmark}} Sep 19 2024 | {{checkmark}} Oct 2024 |

## Business value
When using the Expense Portal, the ability to attach multiple documents to an expense report in one go significantly enhances the efficiency and user experience for employees and finance teams. This feature reduces the time and effort required to submit expense reports, leading to faster processing and reimbursement.

## Feature details
Bulk Attachment Capability: In the Expense Portal, expense users can now select “Add existing documents” and attach multiple documents to an expense report simultaneously, rather than uploading each document individually.
