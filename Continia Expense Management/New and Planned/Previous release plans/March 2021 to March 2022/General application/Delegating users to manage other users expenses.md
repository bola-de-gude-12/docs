# Delegating users to manage other users expenses

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Delegating users to manage other users expenses | {{checkmark}} Mar 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Apr 1, 2021 |-|

## Business value
From Continia Expense Management 2.60 onward, it has been possible for delegated users to create expenses, mileages, and per diems on behalf of others inside Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. In the next version, it will be possible for the delegated users to do this in the Continia Expense App and the Continia Expense Portal, meaning that another level of convenience will be added.

## Feature details
When the creation of expenses, mileages, and per diems has been delegated to a user, it's already possible for this delegated user to carry out these tasks in Business Central on behalf of other users. But with this feature, that functionality will be extended to the Expense App and the Expense Portal, so that delegated users can now create and manage expenses, mileages, and per diems for others in these applications as well.

For more information, refer to the [Delegating Expense Tasks](@EM-166) article.
