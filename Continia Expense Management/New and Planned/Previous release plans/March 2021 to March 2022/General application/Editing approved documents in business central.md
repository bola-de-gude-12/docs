# Editing approved documents in Business Central

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Editing approved documents in Business Central | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2021 |-|



## Business value

This feature provides a more smooth flow by removing the need for reopening documents whenever there’s a need for minor corrections that have no influence on amounts, such as changing G/L account or certain dimensions.

## Feature details
It's now possible to allow selected users to edit approved documents in Expense Management. These users can edit a selection of fields on documents with the status Pending Approval or Released. Modifying fields that influence amounts still requires that the document is reopened.
