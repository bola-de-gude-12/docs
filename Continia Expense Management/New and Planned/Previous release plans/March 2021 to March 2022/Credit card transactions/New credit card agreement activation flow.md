# New credit card agreement activation flow

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| New Credit Card Agreement Activation Flow | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2021 |-|

## Business value
The need for time-consuming communication back and forth with banks is gone with the new activation flow for credit card agreements, making access to transaction feeds quick and efficient. 

## Feature details
In the new activation flow for credit card agreements, users will be asked to provide certain transaction details, and if all conditions are met, Continia's support will activate the agreement.
