# Mileage Receipt VAT Calculation Improvements for the UK Market

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Mileage receipt VAT calculation improvements for the UK market | {{checkmark}} Mar 2023 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2023 |

## Business value
With this improved feature in Expense Management, companies can extract information about claimable VAT from mileage fuel receipts. This information can be used for tax deduction purposes.

## Feature details
Expense Management will help calculate VAT refunds on mileages when receipts for fuel are provided.

For more information, see the full article [Mileage VAT Calculation (UK)](@EM-155)
