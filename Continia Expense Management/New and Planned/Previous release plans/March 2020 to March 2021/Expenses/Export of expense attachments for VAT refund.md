# Export expense attachments for VAT refund

## Feature details

Continia Expense Management 6.50 introduces the ability to export expense attachments, making it easy to handle VAT refunds via third-party service providers like SimNomics and [Nikosax](https://www.nikosax.com/en/our-services/vat-refund).

For more information, refer to the [Posting Expenses](@EM-11#to-export-expense-attachments) article.
