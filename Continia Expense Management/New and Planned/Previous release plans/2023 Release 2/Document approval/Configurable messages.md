# Configurable Messages

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Configurable messages | - | {{checkmark}} Oct 2023 |

## Business value
This feature can speed up the approval process in cases where a submitted expense triggers a warning in Business Central by allowing expenses with certain warning types to get the status "Pending approval". 

## Feature details
Admins can customize their workflow based on warnings, allowing for expense documents with the status "open" to proceed to the approval stage.
