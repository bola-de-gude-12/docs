# AI Receipt Scanner

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| AI Receipt Scanner | - | {{checkmark}} Nov 2022 |

## Business value
This will save expense users time and effort by automating part of the process of adding certain important details – amount, date, currency, and merchant – when creating expenses.

## Feature details
When you take a picture of a receipt, the AI Receipt Scanner will try to identify amount, date, currency, and merchant and automatically add this to your expense. The AI Receipt Scanner will also add data to images added to Saved Images and when you add PDF files from, for example, emails.
