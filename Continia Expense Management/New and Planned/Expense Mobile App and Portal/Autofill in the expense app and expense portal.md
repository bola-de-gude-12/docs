# Autofill in the Expense App and Expense Portal

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Autofill in the Expense App and Expense Portal | - | {{checkmark}} Oct 2023 |

## Business value
This feature speeds up your expense submissions of receipts from the same merchant in the Expense App and Expense Portal. You will benefit from automatic suggestions and, in the app, conveniently highlighted potential options.

## Feature details
When a new expense is created, fields with a look-up value will be automatically filled out with a suggestion based on prior history.
