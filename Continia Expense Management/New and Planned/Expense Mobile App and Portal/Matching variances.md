# Matching variance settings synchronization from Business Central to Continia Online

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Matching variance settings synchronization from Business Central to Continia Online | - | {{checkmark}} Oct 2023 |

## Business value
With the upcoming enhancement to the Expense App/Portal, users can define matching variances, providing greater control and customization in the expense matching process.

## Feature details
To ensure seamless expense matching between bank transactions and expenses, matching variances will be seamlessly integrated into the Expense App/Portal. This enhancement will enable users to apply the same settings for matching expenses as in Business Central, promoting consistency and accuracy throughout the process.
