# Expense Attachments Visible on Allocation Pages in the Mobile Expense App

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Expense attachments visible on allocation pages in the Mobile Expense App | {{checkmark}} Sep 19 2024 | {{checkmark}} Oct 2024 |

## Business value

This feature provides quicker access to attachments, making it easy for expense users to verify details on receipts on the spot instead of having to navigate several pages back. 

## Feature details

Currently, attachments are only displayed on the Expense header page, but with this new feature, expense attachments are also displayed on each allocation page. 

For more information, refer to the [Allocating Expense Costs](@EM-276) article.
