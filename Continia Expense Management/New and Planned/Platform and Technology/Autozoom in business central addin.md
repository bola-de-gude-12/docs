# Autozoom in Business Central Add-In

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Autozoom in Business Central add-in | - | {{checkmark}} October 2023 |

## Business value
This feature ensures you always get a full view of your attached receipt images in the Business Central add-in, which will you save time when you navigate multiple expense documents. You can still use the zoom controls for closer inspection when needed.

## Feature details

Attached receipt images are automatically scaled to the add-in size.
