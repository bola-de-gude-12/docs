# Demo Machines with Local Data and Setup Files

| Feature | Public preview | General availability online |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Demo machines with local data and setup files | {{checkmark}} Sep 19 2024 | {{checkmark}} Oct 2024 |

## Business value

Making demos of localized versions of Expense Management will become much easier with this feature, as it provides local setup data and takes local requirements into account. 

## Feature details

Localized setup files can be downloaded to preproduction and production systems and be used as basis for custom setups.
