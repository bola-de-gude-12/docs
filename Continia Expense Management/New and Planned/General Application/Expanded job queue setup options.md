# Expanded Job Queue Setup Options

| Feature                          |        Public preview       |   General availability   |
| -------------------------------- | :-------------------------: | :----------------------: |
| Expanded job queue setup options | \{{checkmark\}} Sep 19 2024 | \{{checkmark\}} Oct 2024 |

## Business value

With this new feature, it gets much more intuitive to configure job queues. The new Job Queue Page presents a number of setup options that are easily accessible to users, making it quick to get started configuring job queues without needing to browse through documentation for setup procedures.

\


## Feature details

A new action, **Job Queue Setup**, is available in the **Setup** action menu. This allows users to create and remove job queue entries for recurring Expense Management jobs – such as Continia Online synchronizations, field dependency updates, and approval email sending – with just one click.

Synchronization with Continia Online is also carried out automatically if more than 10 minutes have passed without a synchronization after an approval email, reminder email or expense report status has been sent.

For more information, see [Setting up Job Queues](../../../Continia%20Expense%20Management/New%20and%20Planned/General%20Application/@EM-65/#to-use-the-job-queue-setup-page).
