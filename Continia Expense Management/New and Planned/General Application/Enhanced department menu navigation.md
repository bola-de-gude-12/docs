## Business value
An enhanced department menu improves navigation for users by providing streamlined access to Expense Management pages and reports. By integrating direct links to key expense-related functionalities within the department menu, it's easier to locate and access relevant pages, saving time on navigation. This improvement enhances user experience, boosts productivity, and ensures that Expense Management tasks are more accessible for employees and managers.
