# Payment Type

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Payment Type | {{checkmark}} Mar 2022 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2022 | - |

## Business value
The expanded payment options will make payment setups and settings much easier and faster to tailor to the specific needs of your organization.  

## Feature details
Payment options in Expense Management will be massively expanded, making it possible to process petty cash expenses, expenses in connection with credit cards with multiple users, expenses in connection employee cards, and much more as well as to define payment type per user.
