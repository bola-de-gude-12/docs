# Support for the 60-Day Rule

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Support for the 60-Day Rule | {{checkmark}} Mar 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Apr 1, 2021 |-|

## Business value
According to Danish legislation, all employees are entitled to a tax-free mileage allowance from their employers for any business travel using their own private vehicles for up to 60 days within a 12-month period. This previously unsupported rule, known as the "60-day rule", will now be supported.

## Feature details
With this feature, the 60-day rule will be fully supported in Continia Expense Management.
