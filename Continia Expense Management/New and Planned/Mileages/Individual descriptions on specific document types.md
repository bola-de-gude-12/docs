# Individual Descriptions on Specific Document Types

| Feature                                          | General availability on-premises | General availability online |     Public preview     |
| ------------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------: | :-------------------------: | :--------------------: |
| More descriptive labels specific to expense type |      {{checkmark}} Apr 2025      |   {{checkmark}} Apr 2025    | {{checkmark}} Mar 2025 |

## Business value
This update gives companies more control over how they label expenses. By having different captions for Expense, Mileage, and Expense Reports, companies can:

- Tailor captions to suit different expense types, making tracking more precise.
- Use specific terms that align with policies and regulations.
- Make it easier for employees to submit expenses correctly.

## Feature details
This feature allows users to:

- Define captions such as "Description" for expenses and "Purpose" for mileage.
- For increased clarity, use "Attendee" for expenses and "Passenger" for mileage.

For more information, see [Setting up fields](@EM-80).
