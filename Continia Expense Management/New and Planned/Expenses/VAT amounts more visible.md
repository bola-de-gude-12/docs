# VAT amounts more visible

| Feature | General availability on-premises | General availability online | Public preview |
| --- | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| VAT amounts more visible | {{checkmark}} Sep 1, 2021 | {{checkmark}} Oct 1, 2021 |-|

## Business value
Working with expenses with different VAT rates has become significantly easier for the bookkeeper, as VAT amounts are much more prominently displayed. 

## Feature details
New fields containing VAT amount and amount without VAT have been added to the Expense card and the Expense Split and Allocate page.
