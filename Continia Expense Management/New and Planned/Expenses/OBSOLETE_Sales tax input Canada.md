# Sales Tax Input in Canada Continia Expense App

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Sales Tax Input in Canada Continia Expense App |-| Oct 2023 |

## Business value
A new feature in the Canada Continia Expense app allows users to specify tax amounts directly from the initial screen. This enhancement eliminates the need for users to wait for allocations in Business Central, providing a more efficient expense-claiming process for users in Canada.

## Feature details
With this functionality, users will have the ability to manually enter the sales tax amount from their receipts, eliminating the requirement for synchronization with Business Central. This feature will be integrated with OCR recognition, reducing the need for manual entry and enhancing the overall user experience.
