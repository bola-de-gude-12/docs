# Detailed Changelog for Continia Expense Management 2020 R2

This article lists all new features and bug fixes for each version of Continia Expense Management 2020 R2.

> \[!IMPORTANT] This version of Expense Management is no longer supported. As a consequence of this, Continia will no longer correct any errors appearing in this version, and no further service packs relating to the version will be released. For supported versions of Expense Management, see [Software Lifecycle Policy and Continia Expense Management On-Premises](../../../Continia%20Expense%20Management/New%20and%20Planned/Detailed%20changelogs/@EM-104/).

> \[!TIP] As a Continia partner, you can be notified of new Expense Management versions and service packs whenever they're released. To sign up for this service, go to [this page](https://continia.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360005316840-Receive-a-notification-upon-new-Expense-Management-releases) in the Continia PartnerZone (only available to partners).
