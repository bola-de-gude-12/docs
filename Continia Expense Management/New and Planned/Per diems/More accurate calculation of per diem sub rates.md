# Expanded Options for Calculating Per Diem Subrates

| Feature | Public preview | General availability |
| --- | :-: | :-: |
| Expanded options for calculating per diem subrates | {{checkmark}} Mar 2023 | {{checkmark}} Apr 2023 |

## Business value
More accurate application of per diem subrates.

## Feature details
With this feature, you can choose between two options for calculating per diem subrates, Daily or Last Day Only. In previous versions, subrates were applied to the last day as default.
