# Enhanced transparency for per diem calculations

| Feature                            | General availability on-premises | General availability online |     Public preview     |
| ---------------------------------- | :------------------------------: | :-------------------------: | :--------------------: |
| Per diem calculations transparency |      {{checkmark}} Apr 2025      |   {{checkmark}} Apr 2025    | {{checkmark}} Mar 2025 |

## Business value
This feature increases visibility into per diem calculations, empowering users with a clearer understanding of how per diem amounts are determined.

By providing greater transparency, it reduces confusion and streamlines the process of reviewing and approving per diem expenses.

## Feature details
When processing per diem expenses, the system now offers improved insight into the calculation process, with a detailed breakdown of the per diem amount, prominently displayed in a fact box.

This allows users to easily view and verify the factors contributing to the final calculation, ensuring accuracy, and simplifying expense validation directly within the interface. For more information, see [Overview of per diems](@EM-10).
