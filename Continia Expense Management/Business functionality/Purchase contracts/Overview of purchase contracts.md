# Overview of Purchase Contracts

The Purchase Contracts module enables you to manage and keep track of your organization's purchase contracts, subscriptions, and other recurring costs. It allows administrators to have all such contracts and costs gathered in one convenient place, thereby providing them with an excellent overview that makes it easy for them to compare with alternative providers and to determine if, for example, multiple separate contracts may be consolidated into one to reduce the overall cost. Overall, the module facilitates correct invoicing across the board, which typically saves you both time and money.

All contracts can be reviewed easily and on a recurring basis, so that you're always on top of whether they need to be renewed, modified, or canceled. By making it simple for you to assign different reviewers to different contracts, the module enables you to spread the workload while streamlining the process, and you can even have expenses and purchase documents approved automatically by linking them to a purchase contract. In this way, your organization can save hours upon hours of manual labor and hassle, thus freeing up large amounts of time and resources to be spent on other important tasks.

The module works with Continia Document Capture and Continia Expense Management and may involve purchase documents (invoices and credit memos) and/or expenses, each of which can be linked to a purchase contract. The list of different types of contracts and subscriptions that you can favorably manage using the module is virtually endless. It includes, but is in no way limited to, the following:

* Office lease
* Software licenses
* Insurance
* Phone and internet services
* Server backup
* Facility management services, such as catering, cleaning, and indoor plant maintenance
* Car leasing
* Business newspapers and magazines
