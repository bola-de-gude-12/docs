# Enabling or Disabling Allowances for Per Diem

You can customize allowances for per diem. For example, if accommodation is not used, it can be disabled, allowing you to focus only on relevant per diem details.

## To enable or disable allowances for per diem

You enable or disable allowances for per diem by following these steps:

1. Choose the {{search}} icon, enter **Expense Management Setup**, and then choose the related link.
1. In the **Per Diem** FastTab, under **Enable Allowance**, enable or disable the relevant allowances.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Remember to specify rates for all allowances used.
