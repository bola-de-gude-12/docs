# Enabling Multiple Accommodations for Per Diem 

Enabling multiple accommodation types on a per diem allows users the flexibility to choose different lodging options for each day of travel. This feature empowers employees to tailor their accommodations to their needs, promoting greater comfort and convenience during business trips. 

To enable multiple accommodations for per diem:

1. Choose the {{search}} icon, enter **Expense Management Setup**, and then choose the related link.
1. On the **Per Diem** FastTab, select **Enable multiple accommodations**.
