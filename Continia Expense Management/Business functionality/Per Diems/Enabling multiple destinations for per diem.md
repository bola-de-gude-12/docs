# Enabling Multiple Destinations for Per Diem

In numerous companies, multi-leg trips are a common aspect of daily business. For instance, a consultant may visit various clients at different destinations at varying rates. By enabling multiple destinations for per diem, managing rates for these trips becomes much easier.

With this feature, you can specify one or more destinations for each per diem day. The applicable rate will be calculated based on the last destination visited. For the return trip, local rates will apply. 

To enable multiple destinations for per diem:

1. Choose the {{search}} icon, enter **Expense Management Setup**, and then choose the related link.
1. In the **Per Diem** FastTab, select **Enable multiple destinations**.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Remember to [set up per diem rates](@EM-86) for all destinations.
