# Submitting expenses

{{include "submitting-documents"}}

## Sharing attachments directly from mobile apps

As an expense user, you can share attached PDF receipts or invoices with Expense Management straight from your mobile device, and then add them to new or existing expenses in the Expense App.

> [!NOTE]
> This works for most mobile apps that handle/store PDF files, such as email apps, banking apps, Microsoft OneDrive, Uber, Microsoft Teams, or virtually any other app that features a **Share** icon.

To share a PDF receipt or invoice with Expense Management from your mobile device:

1. On your mobile device, go to the app with the PDF attachment that you want to share with Expense Management.
1. Select or open the attachment that you want to share.
1. Tap the app's **Share** icon.
1. In the list of apps available to share to, tap the Expense App, then tap **Send To Expense App Receipts**. The Expense Mobile App opens.
1. A dialog appears, asking if you want to open your list of saved receipts. Tap **Yes** to open the list.
1. From the list, select the shared attachment. This opens up a pop-up menu.
1. From the menu, tap **Add** > **new expense** (to create a new expense based on this attachment) or **existing document** (to add the attachment to an existing document).
1. Fill in all required fields, then submit the expense as usual.

The expense, including the shared attachment, is submitted for approval.
