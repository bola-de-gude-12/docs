# Adding a Business Vendor

Posting to a business vendor is relevant in situations such as when you use a corporate credit card with a vendor that’s already in the system, and you want the transaction to be connected to that vendor in the ledger entry. The functionality is activated by adding a "Vendor No." on an expense.

To add a business vendor, follow these steps:

1. Select the {{search}} icon, enter **Expenses**, and then select the related link.
2. On the action bar, select **Edit**. Note that you can add a business vendor to an expense regardless of the status of the expense. 
3. In the **Posting** FastTab, select **Show more**.
4. Fill in the **Vendor No.** field.

The expense will now be posted to this vendor. As the expense has already been paid for on the credit card, a balanced posting will automatically be created on the vendor. This ensures that the expense doesn't appear as outstanding on the vendor.
