| Version | Release date | Support end date |
| --- | --- | --- |

| Payment Management 2024 R1 (24.0) | Apr 1, 2024 | Apr 1, 2026 |
| Payment Management 2023 R2 (7.0) | Oct 1, 2023 | Oct 1, 2025 |
