The process of downloading {param2} from Continia Online is referred to as "synchronization" within Expense Management. You can initiate synchronization from various locations in the system, including the Expense Management Setup page. 

To perform synchronization:

1. Choose the {{search}} icon, enter **Expense Management Setup**, and then choose the related link.
2. On the action bar, select **Actions** > **Functions** > **Synchronize with Continia Online**.

When the synchronization has been finalized, there will be a confirmation dialog with details about the {param2} that have been downloaded.

Regular synchronization can often be time-consuming. To streamline this process, job queues offer automated execution in the background. For more information about job queues in Expense Management, refer to the [Setting up Job Queues](@EM-65) article.
